# Blade Wolves

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Monster |

The Blade Wolves are a type of monster that roam the First Floor of the Tower. They are known among explorers as a common hunting target, often sought for their value as prey. Little is documented about their specific characteristics, but their name suggests blade-like features, possibly sharp appendages or claws that make them dangerous adversaries. Their presence on the lower floors implies a moderate threat level, suitable for novice hunters seeking to test their skills.

## Appearances

Blade Wolves are mentioned in Chapter 11.0 by a group of explorers discussing potential hunting targets on the First Floor. These explorers reference the monsters as a viable option for their expedition, indicating that Blade Wolves are sufficiently numerous and dangerous to be worth pursuing.
