Ghouls are ninth-rank undead monsters that inhabit the western region of the First Floor. These creatures appear as decaying corpses with rotten skin, empty eye sockets, and sharp claws. They move on all fours, often remaining motionless until prey approaches within a certain range, at which point they charge aggressively.

During an expedition near the boundary of the Goblin District, the adventurer Bjorn encountered a group of Ghouls. Despite their menacing appearance, they proved to be relatively weak opponents, as Bjorn dispatched them easily with a single Hammer Smash. Their limited intelligence and predictable behavior make them a manageable threat for experienced fighters.

## Behavior

Ghouls lie in wait, remaining stationary until a living creature comes within a certain distance. Once triggered, they charge forward on all fours to attack. Their straightforward assault pattern makes them predictable and vulnerable to well-timed counterattacks, as demonstrated when Bjorn defeated them with a single blow.
