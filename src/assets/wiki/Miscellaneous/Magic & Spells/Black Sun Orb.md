The Black Sun Orb is a sixth-rank attack spell of the sun attribute, which combines fire and holy elements. It is considered less powerful than a fireball spell of the same rank, but retains the destructive potential typical of its tier. The orb manifests as a sphere of dark solar energy that explodes on impact, releasing a burst of fire and holy light.

## Properties

The Black Sun Orb is a sixth-rank attack spell that draws upon the sun attribute, merging fire and holy aspects. While potent, its raw destructive force is slightly inferior to a standard fireball of equivalent rank. The spell requires significant magical energy to cast and produces a wide-area explosion upon detonation.

## Notable Usage

During an encounter with a Vampire Duke, the mage Arua Raven cast the Black Sun Orb to harm the undead noble. The explosion successfully damaged the Vampire Duke, but the blast also caught Raven's own party members, stunning them momentarily. This incident highlights the spell's indiscriminate area-of-effect nature and the risks of using it in close-quarters combat.
