# Blade Storm

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Wind Spell |
| **Caster** | Raven |
| **Properties** | |
| Effect | Shreds multiple enemies over a wide area |
| Notable Use | Bloodstained Fortress (bridge and outer walls) |

Blade Storm is a wind spell wielded by Raven, generating a fierce gale that shreds multiple enemies across a wide area. It is a devastating area-of-effect attack, capable of clearing swathes of opponents in a single casting. The spell manifests as a howling torrent of wind, laced with razor-sharp currents that tear through flesh and armor alike. Its most notable deployment occurred during the assault on the Bloodstained Fortress, where it was used to clear the fortress's bridge and outer walls, demonstrating its ability to decisively shift the momentum of a siege.

## History

Blade Storm was employed by Raven during the assault on the Bloodstained Fortress. The spell was used to clear the fortress's bridge and outer walls, sweeping away multiple enemies across a wide area and paving the way for the advancing forces. Its appearance in that battle cemented its reputation as a potent tool for breaking defensive lines.
