# Attribute Enhancement

| | |
|---|---|
| **Spell Name** | Attribute Enhancement |
| **Caster** | Arua Raven |
| **Target** | Black Sun Orb |
| **Properties** | |
| Type | Enhancement |
| Effect | Temporarily increases the power of the targeted orb |

Attribute Enhancement is a spell utilized by Arua Raven to augment the power of the Black Sun Orb. The spell is cast to temporarily increase the orb's effectiveness, allowing it to deliver a more potent strike. Typically employed in high-stakes combat situations, Attribute Enhancement channels raw magical energy into the orb, amplifying its destructive capabilities. The spell's precise incantation and magical formula remain known only to Raven, who has demonstrated its use on at least one occasion. When cast, the Black Sun Orb glows with intensified light, signifying the successful application of the enhancement.

## Usage

Notable instances where Attribute Enhancement has been employed.

### Ch35.0: Pre-Fire Enhancement

Arua Raven cast Attribute Enhancement on the Black Sun Orb just before firing it, significantly boosting the orb's power and effectiveness in the ensuing attack.
