# Wound aggravation curse

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Curse |
| **User** | Raven |
| **Effect** | Aggravates wounds, seals passive abilities |

The Wound Aggravation Curse is a curse spell used by Raven that amplifies the severity of existing injuries and suppresses passive defensive abilities such as Body Preservation. When cast, the curse latches onto open wounds, causing them to bleed more profusely and become more painful, while simultaneously preventing natural healing or regeneration from activating. This makes the curse highly effective against enemies that rely on sustained combat or regenerative traits to outlast opponents. Raven has demonstrated the spell's utility in combat, using it to neutralize the regeneration of Death Fiends, rendering them far more vulnerable to sustained attacks.

## Effect

The curse aggravates wounds by intensifying their damage and pain over time. It also seals passive abilities that would normally mitigate harm or regenerate the target, such as Body Preservation. The curse does not create new wounds but instead worsens existing ones, making each injury more debilitating. The effect persists as long as the curse remains active, requiring either dispelling or the death of the caster to end.

## Notable Uses

Raven employed the Wound Aggravation Curse against Death Fiends in chapter 30. By sealing their regeneration ability, the curse allowed Raven to kill the Death Fiends without them healing from the damage dealt. This use highlights the curse's tactical value against regenerating foes.
