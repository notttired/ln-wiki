# Mark of Grudge

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Curse |
| **Inflicted By** | Banshee |
| **Trigger** | Attacking first |
| **Duration** | Until leaving the Labyrinth |
| **First Appearance** | Chapter 23 |

The Mark of Grudge is a curse invoked by Banshees within the Labyrinth. When an explorer attacks a Banshee first, the creature responds by placing this curse upon the aggressor. Once inflicted, the Banshee relentlessly pursues the cursed individual, singling them out until they either flee the Labyrinth or otherwise escape the monster's range. The curse serves as a deterrent against aggressive tactics, encouraging explorers to avoid initiating combat with Banshees. Experienced explorers such as Bjorn advise caution, warning that engaging a Banshee without provocation invites a targeted and persistent threat.

## Mechanics

The Mark of Grudge is triggered solely by the explorer's first attack on a Banshee. If the explorer does not attack first, the curse is not applied. Once cursed, the Banshee's behavior changes; it becomes fixated on the victim, ignoring other potential targets until the victim leaves the Labyrinth. The curse does not appear to have a duration beyond the victim's presence in the Labyrinth, and leaving the area removes its effects.
