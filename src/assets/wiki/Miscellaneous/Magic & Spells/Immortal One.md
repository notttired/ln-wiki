# Immortal One

The Immortal One is a passive effect that dramatically amplifies a vampire's regenerative capabilities, with the healing rate scaling in direct proportion to the amount of health already lost. This ability was first observed in the Vampire Duke Cambormir, who used it to recover from seemingly fatal wounds during combat. The Immortal One is considered a rare and potent augmentation, as it allows its bearer to endure damage that would otherwise be crippling. Following Cambormir's defeat, the essence of the Immortal One was acquired by Bjorn, granting him the same enhanced regeneration.

## Properties

The Immortal One functions as a passive self-sustaining effect. It does not require activation; rather, it continuously operates in the background, accelerating the body's natural healing processes. The regeneration rate increases as the bearer's health diminishes, making them hardest to kill when at the brink of death. This scaling effect ensures that the ability provides maximum benefit exactly when it is most needed.

## Known Bearers

Individuals who have possessed the Immortal One.

### Cambormir

The Vampire Duke who originally wielded the Immortal One, demonstrating its power in combat by rapidly recovering from critical wounds.

### Bjorn

Acquired the essence of the Immortal One from Cambormir, gaining the same enhanced regenerative abilities.
