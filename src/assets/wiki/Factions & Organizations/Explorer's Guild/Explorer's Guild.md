The Explorer's Guild is a key institution in Rafdonia, offering vital support to adventurers who explore the Labyrinth. Its services include the Binding Spell, which enables party members to begin their journey together, and the Guild serves as a central coordinating body for explorers of all ranks. However, the organization has recently been shaken by a corruption scandal involving its own investigator.

The unjust sentencing of explorer Bjorn Yandel, followed by his dramatic escape and hostage crisis, exposed deep flaws in the Guild's internal processes. The ensuing public outcry forced the Guild to take corrective action, dismissing the corrupt investigator and compensating the wronged explorer. The incident has significantly undermined the Guild's authority and prompted an ongoing investigation into the Crystal Alliance.

## Services

The Explorer's Guild offers various services to support explorers, with the Binding Spell being a prominent example.

### Binding Spell

A magical service that allows party members to start in the same location upon entering the Labyrinth. The cost is 15,000 Stones for two ninth-rank explorers and lasts 24 hours. The fee increases for higher-ranked explorers.

## Controversy

Investigator Coldo Bierman of the Explorer's Guild falsely charged Bjorn Yandel with raiding, denying him a fair verification and sentencing him to death. This action was part of recent internal regulatory changes aimed at low-rank explorers. The unjust sentence led to Bjorn Yandel's prison break and a subsequent hostage crisis at the Guild branch, drawing significant public attention.

## Aftermath

In response to the hostage crisis, the Guild publicly admitted its errors. It dismissed Investigator Coldo Bierman, sentenced Hartz Young to the Execution Ground, and compensated Bjorn Yandel with 5 million Stones along with a rank promotion. The Guild also began an investigation into the Crystal Alliance, a group suspected of involvement in the corruption. The incident has damaged the Guild's reputation and trust within the explorer community.
