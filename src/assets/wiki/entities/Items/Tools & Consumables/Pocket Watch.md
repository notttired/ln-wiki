The pocket watch is a time-telling device originally belonging to the Old Man Hans and now in the possession of Bjorn. It features a distinctive face marked with numbers from 0 to 23, indicating it operates on a 24-hour cycle. Bjorn uses the watch to check the time. The watch's origins and exact craftsmanship are unknown, but its reliable timekeeping suggests it is of decent quality.

## Features

The pocket watch's most notable feature is its dial, which displays numbers from 0 to 23 rather than the common 1-to-12 layout, allowing it to indicate the full 24-hour cycle. This design suggests it may have been crafted for a user who requires precise timekeeping across day and night.
