# Richo Leaf

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Herb |
| **Effect** | Healing wounds |
| **Application** | Direct application to wounds |

Richo Leaf is a herb renowned for its medicinal properties, particularly in healing wounds. Its appearance and effects are recognized by those knowledgeable in herbal remedies, such as the Fairy. When Bjorn encountered the wounded Fairy, he tossed a Richo Leaf to her, which she used to treat her injury. The leaf is typically applied directly to the affected area to facilitate recovery.

## Properties

Richo Leaf possesses healing properties that make it effective for treating wounds. When applied to an injury, it aids in recovery. The Fairy's identification of the leaf underscores its recognized medicinal value.
