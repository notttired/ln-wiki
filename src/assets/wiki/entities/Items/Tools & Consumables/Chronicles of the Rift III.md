# Chronicles of the Rift III

| | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Chronicles of the Rift III |
| **Type** | Book |
| **Current Owner** | Raven |

Chronicles of the Rift III is an ancient tome containing esoteric knowledge pertaining to Rifts and their associated phenomena. The book provides detailed information on the Bloodstained Fortress, including hidden passages within that location. It also describes the method to obtain the Necronomicon from the Demon Worship Hall. Additionally, the volume references the Tear of the Goddess and the Golden Mask, though the instructions for acquiring these artifacts have been partially erased due to weathering over time. Currently, the book is in the possession of Raven, who consults it for research on the Tear of the Goddess, using the surviving text to confirm its origin and effects.

## Contents

The book covers several key locations and artifacts related to Rifts.

### Bloodstained Fortress

Details hidden passages and other knowledge about this rift location.

### Necronomicon

Describes how to obtain this artifact from the Demon Worship Hall.

### Tear of the Goddess

Mentions the artifact and its effect; the acquisition method is partially erased.

### Golden Mask

Mentions the artifact; the acquisition method is partially erased.
