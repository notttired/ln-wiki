# Basilisk Paralysis Poison

| | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Basilisk Paralysis Poison |
| **Type** | Poison |
| **Properties** | |
| Delivery Method | Needle or dagger |
| Effect | Paralysis |
| Antidote | High-grade potion |

Basilisk Paralysis Poison is a potent toxin derived from the basilisk, a creature known for its lethal venom. This poison induces immediate paralysis upon entering the bloodstream, rendering the victim completely immobile while consciousness remains intact. The paralysis is swift and total, affecting both voluntary and involuntary muscles, and can prove fatal if the victim's respiratory system becomes compromised. A high-grade potion is the only known antidote capable of counteracting the poison's effects, though such potions are rare and expensive. The poison is typically administered via a small needle or dagger, allowing for discreet and precise delivery. The Eighth Floor Raider is known to possess and utilize this poison in their operations, deploying it with lethal efficiency against their targets.

## Effects

The poison causes rapid paralysis of the muscular system, including the diaphragm, leading to asphyxiation if left untreated. Victims retain full awareness of their surroundings but are unable to move or speak. The paralysis is reversible only by administering a high-grade potion; no other remedies are known to be effective.

## Notable Usage

During an encounter on the eighth floor of the Tower, the Eighth Floor Raider deployed the poison against two individuals: Ainar, who was struck in the wrist, and Bjorn, who was struck in the spine. Both were immediately paralyzed, demonstrating the poison's rapid onset and potency regardless of the location of the wound.
