The Compendium of Ninth-Rank Essences is a reference work that catalogs the abilities associated with Essences harvested from monsters of the Ninth Rank. Unlike many such tomes that provide exact numerical values, this compendium employs a qualitative tier system—Low, Mid, and High—to classify stat ranges. According to the compendium, a stat value of 21 or higher qualifies as Mid tier, with High tier corresponding to even greater thresholds not detailed in its pages. This makes the book a valuable but preliminary guide for hunters and scholars seeking to understand the potential of Ninth-Rank Essences without access to precise measurements.

The compendium is known to have been consulted by the hunter Bjorn during his analysis of Essence capabilities. He relied on its tier descriptions to interpret the strength of various abilities, highlighting the practical utility of the work in the field. However, the compendium explicitly notes that information on Eighth Rank and above must be obtained separately from the Explorer's Guild, indicating that its scope is deliberately limited to the Ninth Rank. This restriction underscores the hierarchical nature of Essence knowledge, where higher ranks command greater secrecy and cost.

## Stat Tier System

The compendium categorizes Essence stats into three qualitative tiers: Low, Mid, and High. A stat value of 21 or more is marked as Mid tier. Information on Essences of Rank 8 and above is not included; such data must be purchased from the Explorer's Guild.
