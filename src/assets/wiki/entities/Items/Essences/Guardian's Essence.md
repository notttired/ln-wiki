The Guardian's Essence is a special magical essence obtained from the Guardian of a Rift, a powerful entity that spawns within dimensional rifts. It is a rare and valuable loot item sought after by adventurers and essence traders. The essence was claimed by the necromancer Raven during a loot negotiation following the defeat of a Guardian. Later, in the Bloodstained Fortress, Raven agreed to trade the Guardian's Essence to Bjorn in exchange for a Corpse Golem Essence, highlighting its use as a bargaining chip in the essence trade.

## Background

Guardian's Essence is dropped exclusively by the Guardian of a Rift, a formidable creature that serves as the final obstacle within certain dimensional rifts. The essence is highly prized for its potency and rarity, making it a key item in negotiations between essence collectors and crafters. Raven secured this essence as part of her share of loot from a successful rift incursion.

## Trade History

Raven's possession of the Guardian's Essence led to a notable trade at the Bloodstained Fortress. She met with Bjorn, a fellow essence enthusiast, and they agreed to a direct exchange: the Guardian's Essence for a Corpse Golem Essence. This transaction underscores the dynamic marketplace of rare essences and the value placed on acquiring specific types for various necromantic and alchemical purposes.
