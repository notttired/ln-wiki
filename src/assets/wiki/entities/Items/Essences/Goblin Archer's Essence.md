The Goblin Archer's Essence is a rare consumable item within the Dungeon & Stone system, dropped exclusively by Goblin Archers. These essences contain a fragment of the monster's innate abilities, which can be absorbed by a human to gain a portion of its power. The essence is distinguished by its balanced enhancement of attributes related to archery and stealth, making it highly sought after by rangers and scouts. Bjorn acquired the essence after defeating a Goblin Archer in chapter 13. Recognizing its value to a stealth-oriented companion, he offered it to Erwen as part of a deal. Erwen absorbed the essence in chapter 14, immediately feeling the influx of goblin archer traits. Her senses sharpened, her movements became more fluid, and she gained access to two specialized skills: Poison Arrow, which adds lingering venom to her shots, and Thief's Step, an active ability that renders her partially invisible, especially effective when she remains stationary. Since absorption, the essence has permanently enhanced Erwen's physical capabilities, boosting attributes such as agility, flexibility, vision, scent, awareness, concealment, accuracy, poison resistance, and tenacity. These enhancements, combined with the new skills, have made her a more formidable and elusive combatant.

## Background

Essences are the core skill system of Dungeon & Stone, allowing adventurers to absorb a portion of a monster's abilities upon defeating it. The Goblin Archer's Essence is a rare drop from Goblin Archers, creatures known for their exceptional accuracy and stealthy hunting tactics. Bjorn obtained this essence after slaying a Goblin Archer in chapter 13 and later offered it to Erwen as part of a bargain.

## Skills Granted

Upon absorption, the essence unlocks two distinct skills that reflect the goblin archer's combat style.

### Poison Arrow

A passive skill that imbues any bow-type weapon with poison damage. Arrows fired while this skill is active inflict additional poison damage over time, making it effective for sustained combat.

### Thief's Step

An active skill that grants the user a stealth state, allowing them to become partially invisible. The effect is more pronounced when the user remains still, making it ideal for ambushes and reconnaissance.
