The Vampire Guardian Essence is a rare Guardian's Essence obtained from the Vampire Duke Cambormir within the Bloodstained Fortress. As a Guardian's Essence, it embodies the concentrated power of a high-ranking vampire, granting substantial enhancements to its absorber. After Bjorn Yandel defeated Cambormir, the essence came into his possession. Upon examination by Raven, the essence's precise attributes were revealed.

The essence provides a broad spectrum of stat increases, significantly boosting Natural Regeneration, Strength, Agility, Magic Resistance, Physical Resistance, Soul Power, Mana Sensitivity, and Dark Resistance. However, it imposes a notable weakness to sunlight, decreasing Sunlight Resistance. In addition to these passive enhancements, the essence grants four unique skills: Source of Darkness (Passive), Sacrificial Mark (Active), Immortal One (Active), and Blood Master (Active). The full capabilities of these skills remain largely undocumented, though their names hint at themes of darkness, sacrifice, immortality, and blood manipulation.

Bjorn Yandel's absorption of the Vampire Guardian Essence marks a significant milestone in his growth, bestowing upon him the traits of a vampiric guardian without the full drawbacks of vampirism. The essence's influence on his combat abilities and resilience is expected to be profound, though the exact nature of its synergy with his existing powers is still unfolding.

## Background

The Vampire Guardian Essence was retrieved by Bjorn Yandel after slaying the Vampire Duke Cambormir in the Bloodstained Fortress. Raven performed an analysis of the essence, revealing its complete stat profile and associated skills in chapter 44.
