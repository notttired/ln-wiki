The Corpse Golem Essence is a potent but flawed enhancement item harvested from the Corpse Golem of the Bloodstained Fortress. It embodies the golem's composite nature, granting significant improvements to physical resilience and strength at the cost of perception and agility. The essence increases Pain Resistance, Bone Density, and Poison Resistance, along with a notable boost to raw Strength, but imposes penalties to Perception and increases Appetite and Weight. These attributes make it desirable for warriors seeking durability but unsuitable for scouts or those reliant on speed.

The essence also bestows two unique skills derived from the golem's flesh. Acidic Fluid causes the user's blood to become acidic, damaging any equipment that comes into contact with wounds. Flesh Explosion allows the user to detonate a portion of their own body at the cost of hit points, dealing area damage. These abilities reflect the golem's grotesque and self-destructive combat style.

In the aftermath of the Corpse Golem's defeat, the essence came into the possession of Bjorn, who later traded it to Raven in exchange for the Guardian's Essence, a transaction that transferred the essence's power to a new wielder.

## Acquisition

Dropped by the Corpse Golem in the Bloodstained Fortress. Later traded by Bjorn to Raven in exchange for the Guardian's Essence.

## Attributes

The essence grants an array of physical enhancements and drawbacks. It increases Pain Resistance by 70, Bone Density by 55, Poison Resistance by 12, and Strength by 15. However, it reduces Perception by 5, increases Appetite by 9, and adds 21 units of Weight. These changes reflect the golem's durable but lumbering nature.

## Skills

The essence bestows two special abilities derived from the Corpse Golem's physiology.

### Acidic Fluid

Passive skill: The user's blood becomes acidic, damaging any equipment that comes into contact with wounds.

### Flesh Explosion

Active skill: The user can detonate a portion of their own body at the cost of hit points, dealing area damage.
