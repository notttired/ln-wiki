The Necronomicon is a tome of Dark Magic discovered by Raven within the stone chamber of the Bloodstained Fortress's Demon Worship Hall. The book was concealed behind a hidden mechanism, and Raven claimed it as her spoils after uncovering its location. Unlike many magical artifacts in the fortress, the Necronomicon can be removed from the premises without the use of Distortion Magic, suggesting a unique property that allows it to bypass the area's magical restrictions. The tome is considered a valuable item for inventory purposes and is known to contain powerful Dark Magic, making it a sought-after object for those who dabble in forbidden arts.

## Properties

The Necronomicon is imbued with Dark Magic and is treated as a standard inventory item. It can be transported outside the Bloodstained Fortress without the need for Distortion Magic, indicating a lack of spatial anchoring or special binding to the location.
