The Guardian's Wristguard is a Numbers Item bearing the designation No. 3112, renowned for its immense value of approximately 50 million Stone. As a Numbers Item, it belongs to a class of rare and powerful artifacts that are highly sought after by hunters and adventurers. The wristguard is exclusively obtainable from Rifts located on the Fourth Floor or higher, making it a prize reserved for those who venture into the deeper, more dangerous levels of the Tower.

Hikurod secured the Guardian's Wristguard during an expedition into the Bloodstained Fortress, a perilous location within the Tower. Its reveal impressed Bjorn, a seasoned hunter, highlighting the item's prestige and rarity. The acquisition of such a valuable Numbers Item marked a significant achievement for Hikurod, demonstrating his capability and fortune in navigating the treacherous environments of the higher floors.

As a Numbers Item, the Guardian's Wristguard is not only a symbol of wealth but also a testament to the wielder's prowess. Its exact properties remain veiled, but its exclusive drop conditions and astronomical price underscore its status as a coveted treasure among those who seek power and riches in the Tower.

## Background

Numbers Items are a class of rare artifacts with unique properties, each bearing a distinct number. They are highly valued for their potency and scarcity, often sought after by elite hunters. The Guardian's Wristguard is one such item, with the number 3112.

## History

The Guardian's Wristguard was discovered by Hikurod in the Bloodstained Fortress, a location on the higher floors of the Tower. Its revelation during a conversation with Bjorn captured the attention of the veteran hunter, making it a notable piece in Hikurod's collection.

## Rarity

The wristguard is valued at 50 million Stone and can only be found in Rifts on the Fourth Floor or above. This extreme rarity contributes to its high worth and desirability among collectors and fighters.
