The Tear of the Goddess is a one-time use Sacred Artifact of unknown origin. When destroyed, it releases a powerful Grace that can heal allies or inflict immense damage upon Evil Attribute monsters. The artifact was first recorded 150 years ago when Cardinal Androne used it to save the Pope's life, after which it vanished from the world. In the present, the adventurer Bjorn discovered a Tear of the Goddess within a broken fountain statue in the courtyard of the Bloodstained Fortress. Recognizing its potential, he kept it hidden in his boot for emergency use. During the confrontation with the Vampire Duke, Bjorn forced the artifact into the vampire's mouth and crushed it, unleashing its Grace. The divine energy temporarily sealed the Vampire Duke's resistances and caused extensive internal damage, turning the tide of battle. The artifact was destroyed in the process. Raven, having learned of Bjorn's use of the Tear of the Goddess, has since begun investigating the item and its historical significance.

## History

The Tear of the Goddess was used 150 years ago by Cardinal Androne to save the Pope. Following that event, the artifact vanished from record, leading many to believe it was lost. Its origin remains unknown.

## Discovery

Bjorn discovered the Tear of the Goddess inside a broken fountain statue in the courtyard of the Bloodstained Fortress. After escaping the vampire, he retrieved the artifact and concealed it within his boot for later use.

## Properties

The Tear of the Goddess is classified as a Sacred Artifact. It is a one-time use item that, when destroyed, releases a divine Grace. This Grace can heal an ally or inflict severe damage on Evil Attribute creatures, depending on the context of its activation.

## Usage

Bjorn used the Tear of the Goddess against the Vampire Duke by forcing it into the vampire's mouth and destroying it. The resultant Grace temporarily nullified the Vampire Duke's resistances and caused extensive internal damage, proving critical in the confrontation.
