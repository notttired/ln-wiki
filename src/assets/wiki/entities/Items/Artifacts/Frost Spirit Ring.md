# Frost Spirit Ring

| | |
|---|---|

| **Rarity** | Rare |

The Frost Spirit Ring is a Numbers Item catalogued as number 9425, valued at approximately 1.5 million Stones. This rare ring possesses a unique property: it can seal the skills of one absorbed Essence while preserving the stat bonuses provided by that Essence. The sealing effect is reversible; removing the ring reactivates the sealed skills. This feature is particularly favored by early-stage explorers who prioritize raw attribute gains over the combination of skills. The ring came into Bjorn's possession as compensation from Deian, serving as a substitute for a portion of a 5 million Stones debt.

## Effect

The ring seals the skills of a single absorbed Essence, preventing their use while retaining the stat bonuses. This allows explorers to benefit from enhanced attributes without managing complex skill sets. The seal can be undone at any time by removing the ring, restoring full access to the Essence's skills.

## Acquisition

Deian offered the Frost Spirit Ring to Bjorn as part of a compensation package, substituting for a 5 million Stones payment. Bjorn accepted the ring as a satisfactory settlement.
