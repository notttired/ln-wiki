# Numbers Item

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Item |
| **Origin** | Guardian's spoils in a Rift |
| **First Appearance** | Chapter 29 |

The Numbers Item is a mysterious and valuable artifact recovered from the spoils of a Guardian encountered within a Rift. Its exact nature and function remain unknown to most, as it has only been mentioned in passing. The dwarf Murad specifically insisted on claiming this item from the Guardian's hoard, suggesting that it holds significant worth or utility, particularly for those with knowledge of its properties. Whether the item possesses numerical inscriptions, grants numeric abilities, or is simply named for its rarity is unclear, but its acquisition was a point of contention during the division of spoils.

## Background

The Numbers Item was part of the loot obtained from a Guardian defeated in a Rift. Guardians are powerful entities that guard valuable treasures, and the Numbers Item was among the spoils. The dwarf Murad, recognizing its value, insisted on taking it for himself during the allocation of rewards after the battle.

## Significance

Murad's insistence on acquiring the Numbers Item highlights its importance. Dwarves are known for their appreciation of precious metals and gems, but also for their skill in crafting and identifying rare items. This item may be of particular interest to them, possibly due to its material composition, magical properties, or potential for crafting high-quality equipment.
