The House of Baron Martoan is a minor noble house within the kingdom of Rafdonia. Little is known of its history or holdings, as it appears only in passing mention. The house is noted for its distant connection to Dwalki, who claims relation through his aunt's marriage to a member of the house. This tenuous link is the only recorded interaction between an outside figure and the baronial family.

## Background

The House of Baron Martoan holds baronial rank, placing it among the lower tier of Rafdonian nobility. It is not known to wield significant political or military influence, and its lands and titles are not documented in any available records. The house's obscurity suggests it is a quiet, rural domain with little involvement in the affairs of the kingdom.

## Relationships

The only known relationship involving the House of Baron Martoan is its distant connection to Dwalki.

### Dwalki

A traveler and self-proclaimed relative, Dwalki claims a distant kinship to the House of Baron Martoan via his aunt's marriage. The nature and legitimacy of this connection remain unverified, and no other members of the house are known to have acknowledged it.
