The Mage Tower is a prominent organization of mages based in the city of Rafdonia. It serves as a central authority for magical matters, particularly in legal proceedings where mages are called upon to verify innocence or provide expert testimony. The Tower dispatches mages to support its branches when official requests are made, as seen when a Sixth-Rank Mage was sent to assist a branch in need.

The organization's headquarters is a striking black spire that rises high into the city's skyline. Due to local regulations prohibiting windows in tall buildings, the tower is completely windowless. It comprises thirty-one floors, with each level dedicated to various functions and accommodations for its members. The topmost floor houses the personal laboratory of Sixth-Rank Mage Arua Raven, a testament to the Tower's investment in high-ranking researchers.

## Headquarters

The Mage Tower operates from a tall black spire in Rafdonia. The building rises thirty-one stories into the sky and is entirely windowless, conforming to city regulations that prohibit windows in high structures. The uppermost floor, the thirty-first, contains the personal laboratory of Sixth-Rank Mage Arua Raven.

## Functions

The Mage Tower serves as a judicial resource, providing mages to verify claims of innocence in legal disputes. It also responds to support requests from its branches, dispatching high-ranking mages such as Sixth-Rank Mage Arua Raven to assist with local matters.
