# Altemion School

| | |
|---|---|
| **School Name** | Altemion School |
| **Type** | Mage School |

Altemion School is a mage school where aspiring magic users receive formal training in the arcane arts. It serves as the educational institution for Arua Raven, a student who is known to be affiliated with the school. The school's curriculum and specific disciplines are not widely detailed, but its reputation as a place of magical learning is acknowledged in the wider world.
