# Crystal Alliance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Explorer Clan |
| **Status** | Dismantled |

The Crystal Alliance was an Explorer Clan that established a wide communication network using Message Stones, allowing its members to coordinate efforts across the First Floor and beyond. The clan operated as a collective of explorers, leveraging this network for efficient information sharing and pursuit operations. However, the Alliance became embroiled in corruption and scandal following the false accusation against the prominent explorer Bjorn Yandel. Executive Hartz Young, a high-ranking member of the Alliance, personally attacked the elf Erwen, resulting in severe injury when she defended herself and severed his genitals. In retaliation, the Crystal Alliance placed a bounty on Erwen, raising the reward to 20,000 Stone, and used their Message Stones to track her movements. The situation escalated when Bjorn Yandel demanded that the Explorer's Guild investigate the Crystal Alliance for their role in the false testimony provided by Hartz Young. Ultimately, the Alliance was dismantled due to the corruption and ties to the false accusation, marking the end of their operations.

## Background

The Crystal Alliance was founded as an Explorer Clan, a group of adventurers who delve into the depths of the Tower. Their primary innovation was the establishment of a communication network utilizing Message Stones, which allowed members to relay information over a range of approximately 300 meters. This network enabled the clan to coordinate large-scale operations and maintain contact during expeditions.

## Communication Method

The Crystal Alliance's signature tool is the Message Stone, a communication device that facilitates short-range transmission of messages.

### Message Stone

A stone capable of transmitting messages to other linked stones within a 300-meter radius.

## Modus Operandi

The Crystal Alliance employs coordinated pursuit tactics, using Message Stones to relay the location of a target to nearby operatives. Members converge on the target from multiple directions, making escape difficult. This method was notably used in their hunt for Erwen across the First Floor.

## Leadership

The executive Hartz Young served as a high-ranking leader within the Crystal Alliance. He oversaw the bounty on Erwen, increasing the reward to 20,000 Stone and claiming personal rights to her capture. His actions led to the false testimony that implicated Bjorn Yandel.

## Downfall

The Crystal Alliance's downfall began when Bjorn Yandel demanded an official investigation into the clan's involvement in the false testimony. The Explorer's Guild probe uncovered widespread corruption within the organization, ultimately leading to its dismantling. News of the disbandment spread among explorers, marking the end of the Alliance's influence.
