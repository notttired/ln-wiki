# Red Beast Tribe

| | |

The Red Beast Tribe is one of the five great beast tribes, a collection of Beastkin communities recognized for their strength and influence. The tribe is known for its fierce warriors and deep connection to nature. Misha Karlstein, a renowned explorer, is the princess of this tribe, representing its noble lineage and adventurous spirit.

## Background

The Red Beast Tribe holds a prominent position among the five great tribes, and its members are respected across the land. As the tribe's princess, Misha Karlstein embodies the tribe's values of exploration and courage. The tribe's exact territory and customs remain largely unknown outside of Beastkin circles.
