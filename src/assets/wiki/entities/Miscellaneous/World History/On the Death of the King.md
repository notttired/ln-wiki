On the Death of the King is a historical tome detailing the demise of the First King Ravigion III, an event that occurred 150 years prior to the current era. The book serves as a critical piece of evidence confirming that the timeline has advanced by a century and a half from the starting point of the game world. Its contents provide an account of the king's death, including the circumstances and aftermath, though the full details remain obscure to most readers. The work is referenced in Chapter 19, where its existence and content are used to substantiate the temporal shift.

## Historical Significance

The book is primarily known for its role in establishing the timeline. By recording the death of King Ravigion III as having occurred exactly 150 years ago, it provides a concrete anchor point that proves the world has advanced significantly from the beginning of the game. This revelation is pivotal for characters seeking to understand the current state of affairs and the passage of time.

## Contents

On the Death of the King is believed to contain a detailed narrative of the king's final days, including political intrigue, the succession crisis, and the subsequent consolidation of power. While the exact text is not widely circulated, its existence alone carries substantial historical weight.
