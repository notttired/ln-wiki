The Dimensional Collapse is a catastrophic event that occurred 150 years ago, marking a pivotal moment in history. During this incident, Cardinal Androne employed the Tears of the Goddess, a powerful artifact, to save the Pope from an unspecified peril. The event is recorded in the Chronicles of the Rift III, where it serves as a testament to Androne's actions.

The exact nature and cause of the Dimensional Collapse remain shrouded in mystery, as chroniclers have only preserved the outcome rather than the full sequence of events. What is known is that the Collapse had significant repercussions, altering the political and magical landscape of the era. The use of the Tears of the Goddess, an artifact of immense power, underscores the severity of the threat faced by the Pope and the lengths to which Cardinal Androne was willing to go.

Today, the Dimensional Collapse is studied by scholars as an example of desperate measures taken in times of crisis. Its legacy endures in the religious and historical texts that recount Androne's bravery and the artifact's pivotal role. The event remains a cautionary tale about the fragility of reality and the high cost of salvation.
