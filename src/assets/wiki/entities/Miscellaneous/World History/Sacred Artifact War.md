# Sacred Artifact War

The Sacred Artifact War is a historical conflict documented in ancient texts, particularly noted for the involvement of Spirit Engraving and the Fairy Tribe. The war's exact causes and outcomes remain obscure, but it is referenced in scholarly works. Bjorn, a knowledgeable figure, cites a book describing Spirit Engraving's role in the war against the Fairy Tribe when speaking to the Tribe Chief. This suggests that the war pitted users of Spirit Engraving against the Fairy Tribe, though further details are sparse.

## Historical Accounts

The primary source of knowledge about the Sacred Artifact War comes from ancient books.
