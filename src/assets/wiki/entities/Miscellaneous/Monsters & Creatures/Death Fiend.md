The Death Fiend is an eighth-rank undead monster native to the Land of the Dead, most commonly encountered on the second floor. Standing over three meters tall, its distorted humanoid form features natural weapons: one arm is shaped like a blade, the other like a shield. This monster possesses the passive ability Body Preservation, granting rapid regeneration, and can summon lesser undead through its active skill Call of the Dead. Its high defense and regenerative capabilities make it a formidable opponent, though its poor balance leaves it vulnerable to coordinated takedowns.

The Death Fiend's Magic Stone serves a specific purpose beyond its value as a core: when used as a sacrifice, it opens the Bloodstained Fortress Rift on the First Floor of the Land of the Dead. Additionally, the monster may rarely drop an Essence containing both the Call of the Dead and Body Preservation skills, making it a sought-after quarry for those seeking to acquire undead abilities.

## Appearance

The Death Fiend stands over three meters in height with a distorted humanoid form. Its arms are natural weapons: one shaped like a blade, the other like a shield.

## Abilities

The Death Fiend possesses both active and passive skills that make it dangerous in combat.

### Active Skills



#### Call of the Dead

Allows the Death Fiend to summon Ghouls to aid it in battle.

### Passive Skills



#### Body Preservation

Grants rapid regeneration, making it difficult to kill without focused attacks to the head.

## Combat Strategy

Due to its poor balance, the Death Fiend is vulnerable to takedowns. Two Barbarians working together can slam it to the ground and continuously attack its head to overcome its regeneration and defense. This strategy proved effective for Bjorn and Ainar, who hunted over seventy Death Fiends over two days.

## Drops

Death Fiends yield valuable materials. Its Magic Stone can be used as a sacrifice to open the Bloodstained Fortress Rift on the First Floor. Rarely, it drops an Essence containing the Call of the Dead and Body Preservation skills.

### Magic Stone

Used as a sacrifice to open the Bloodstained Fortress Rift on the First Floor.

### Essence

A rare drop containing the skills Call of the Dead and Body Preservation.
