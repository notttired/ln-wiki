Vampire Duke Cambormir is an Advanced Mutant vampire of the Fifth Rank who serves as the Guardian of the Bloodstained Fortress. Unlike lesser monsters, he possesses a name, intelligence, and a history, greeting Bjorn's party with words upon awakening. His combat prowess combines close-quarters brutality with a versatile arsenal of Dark Magic, making him a formidable foe capable of adapting to varied threats.

Cambormir's abilities include Wraith Form for evasion and pursuit, Materialization to manifest his physical form, and the power to transform into explosive crows. He also wields the Sacrificial Mark, a buff that designates victims within a hundred meters to enhance his physical capabilities, and Pain Sharing, a skill that transfers damage he receives back onto the attacker. His Immortal One trait grants him extreme regeneration proportional to his lost health, allowing him to withstand catastrophic injuries and prolong battles.

Ultimately, Cambormir fell to Bjorn's relentless assault. Despite incapacitating the entire party with Pain Sharing after Raven's Black Sun Orb detonated, his heart was destroyed by repeated uses of Flesh Explosion, proving that even an immortal vampire can be overcome by determined foes.

## Background

Cambormir replaced the Death Knight as the Guardian of the Bloodstained Fortress. His appearance came as a surprise to Bjorn's party due to his intelligence and conversational ability. As an Advanced Mutant vampire, he possesses a unique history and identity among the fortresses' defenders.

## Abilities

Cambormir's combat style relies on a combination of Dark Magic, physical mutations, and unique skills gained from essences.

### Dark Magic

Includes Wraith Form (becoming intangible to evade attacks) and Materialization (solidifying his body for offense).

### Crow Transformation

Allows Cambormir to turn into a flock of crows, which can explode on contact with enemies.

### Sacrificial Mark

A buff skill that designates sacrifices within 100 meters, increasing his physical abilities proportionally to the number of marked targets.

### Pain Sharing

An active skill obtained from an Essence that shares any damage Cambormir receives with the attacker, making direct confrontation dangerous.

### Immortal One

A regeneration trait that greatly increases healing speed in proportion to lost health, allowing him to survive massive injuries and sustain prolonged fights.
