# Guardian

|---|---|

The Guardian is the boss monster of the Bloodstained Fortress Rift, a dungeon whose contents shift unpredictably between expeditions. It is known to manifest in different forms, and in one notable instance, the expected Death Knight was replaced by the vampire Duke Cambormir, a Rank 5 Advanced Mutant. The Guardian's true nature remains elusive, and its form determines the difficulty and rewards of the encounter. Raven has shown particular interest in the Guardian's spoils, demanding them for her research after the expedition that featured Duke Cambormir.

## Encountered Forms

The Guardian has been known to take on different appearances across expeditions, with each form presenting unique challenges.

### Death Knight

The anticipated form during one expedition, though it did not ultimately manifest.

### Vampire Duke Cambormir

The actual form encountered in the same expedition, identified as a Rank 5 Advanced Mutant.
