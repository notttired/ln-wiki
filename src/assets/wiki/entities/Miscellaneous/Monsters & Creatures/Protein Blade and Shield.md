# Protein Blade and Shield

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Biological Weapons |
| **Part of** | Death Fiend |

The Protein Blade and Shield are a pair of natural weapons that form part of the Death Fiend's anatomy. One of the creature's arms has developed into a long, curved blade resembling a crescent moon, while the other arm has transformed into a shield-like structure. Both are composed entirely of the Death Fiend's own biological tissue, making them an integral part of its body rather than separate implements. These armaments are used in combat, allowing the Death Fiend to both attack and defend without requiring external weaponry.

## Components

The Protein Blade and Shield consist of two distinct arm-mounted appendages.

### Protein Blade

The Death Fiend's right arm is shaped into a long, curved blade reminiscent of a crescent moon. It is used for slashing and piercing attacks.

### Protein Shield

The Death Fiend's left arm has developed into a shield-like form, capable of blocking incoming blows and projectiles.
