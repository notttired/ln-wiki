# Pain Sharing

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Mental Spell |
| **Caster** | Vampire Duke Cambormir |
| **Target(s)** | Murad, Ainar, Porter |
| **Properties** | |
| Effect Type | Pain Sharing |
| Range | Unknown |
| Duration | Brief (during combat) |

Pain Sharing is a mental spell employed by the Vampire Duke Cambormir. Upon casting, it creates a sympathetic link between the caster and the target, causing the target to experience the same pain the caster endures. The spell does not inflict direct physical damage but instead leverages the caster's own injuries as a weapon. After sustaining a heavy blow from the Black Sun Orb, Cambormir activated Pain Sharing, transferring the agony of his wounds to his opponents. The sudden and overwhelming pain incapacitated those linked, rendering them unable to continue the fight.

## Effect

The spell establishes a pain link between the caster and the target. When the caster is injured, the target feels an equivalent degree of pain. The effect is purely sensory and does not cause physical harm, but the intensity can stun or temporarily disable the victim. The duration appears to be brief, but the shock can be debilitating in combat.

## Notable Appearances

During the confrontation in Chapter 35, the Vampire Duke Cambormir used Pain Sharing after being struck by the Black Sun Orb. The spell simultaneously affected Murad, Ainar, and the Porter, stunning them and halting their advance.
