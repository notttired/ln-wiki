# Curse Dispelling

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Curse Removal Magic |
| **Caster** | Raven |
| **First Appearance** | Chapter 30 |
| **Properties** | |
| Effect | Removes magical curses, including petrification |
| Target | Cursed individuals or objects |

Curse Dispelling is a specialized form of magic employed by Raven to nullify and remove magical curses. This spell is particularly effective against transformative curses that alter the physical state of a target, such as petrification. During the Gargoyle encounter in the cursed ruins, Raven demonstrated the spell's utility by successfully lifting the petrification curse from several Gargoyle Statues, restoring them to their original forms and neutralizing the threat they posed. The spell appears to function by unraveling the magical bindings that constitute a curse, though its exact mechanics remain known only to Raven. Its use suggests a deep understanding of curse magic and the ability to counteract even potent enchantments.

## Notable Uses

The spell has been observed in action during a critical encounter, showcasing its practical application in curse removal.

### Gargoyle Encounter

In Chapter 30, Raven used Curse Dispelling to lift the petrification curse from Gargoyle Statues, freeing them from their stone forms and resolving the immediate danger.
