# Lightning Discharge

Lightning Discharge is a rare transformation-type Supernatural Ability utilized by Hikurod Murad. The ability allows the user to release accumulated energy, gained through other abilities, outward in a controlled burst. This discharge is typically channeled through an existing 'Lightning' ability, creating a stunning effect that immobilizes foes briefly. The technique requires precise timing and sufficient stored energy, making it a tactical tool rather than a primary offensive measure.

Murad demonstrated Lightning Discharge during an encounter with a Vampire Duke, where he unleashed the stored energy to stun the powerful undead. The momentary paralysis created an opening for his ally Bjorn to deliver a decisive attack. While not independently lethal, the ability's utility in combat lies in its capacity to disrupt enemy actions and enable coordinated strikes.

The ability's rarity stems from its dual nature as both a storage and release mechanism. Users must first accumulate energy through other abilities, a process that demands careful resource management. Lightning Discharge exemplifies a strategic approach to supernatural combat, emphasizing synergy and teamwork over raw power.

## Mechanics

Lightning Discharge functions as a release mechanism for energy accumulated through other abilities. The user must first store energy, typically via electrical or similar abilities, and then channel it outward in a concentrated burst. The release is not inherently damaging but delivers a powerful stunning effect that can temporarily paralyze targets. The process requires precise control to avoid premature discharge or energy waste.

## Notable Uses



### Stunning the Vampire Duke

During an encounter with a Vampire Duke, Murad used Lightning Discharge to stun the undead noble, creating a momentary opening. This allowed his companion Bjorn to attack and exploit the opportunity, demonstrating the ability's tactical value in team combat.
