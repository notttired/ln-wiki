# Flesh Explosion

| | |
|---|---|
| **Real Name** | Flesh Explosion |
| **Type** | Supernatural Ability |
| **Properties** | |
| Effect | Detonates the user's own flesh |
| User | Bjorn |

Flesh Explosion is a supernatural ability that enables its user to detonate their own flesh at will. This self-destructive power requires the user to possess rapid regeneration or undead physiology to survive the repeated immolation. The ability manifests as a violent burst of energy and gore from the point of detonation, with enough force to shatter iron bars and melt metal doors. Bjorn, a vampire, has demonstrated extensive use of Flesh Explosion during his escape from the Guild's imprisonment, employing it both as a tool for demolition and as a weapon against his foes.

The ability appears to be channeled through the user's body, allowing precise targeting of the explosion. When used in close quarters, the concussive blast and superheated flesh can inflict severe damage on enemies, as seen when Bjorn repeatedly blasted the chest of a Vampire Duke until its heart was destroyed. The effectiveness of the ability is contingent on the user's durability and regenerative capability; without such traits, the user would be left vulnerable or killed by their own power.

## Mechanics

Flesh Explosion allows the user to detonate portions of their own body, generating a powerful explosive force. The ability requires the user to possess a means of surviving the self-inflicted damage, such as vampiric regeneration or similar supernatural resilience. The explosion can be directed, enabling the user to target specific objects or enemies with the blast. The resulting gore and heat are capable of melting ordinary metals and shattering stone.

## Known Uses

Bjorn has employed Flesh Explosion in a series of precise applications during his prison break and subsequent revenge against the Guild. Each use demonstrates the versatility and destructive potential of the ability.

### Breaking Cell Bars

Bjorn used Flesh Explosion to blast through the iron bars of his prison cell, initiating his escape from Guild headquarters.

### Melting the Interrogation Room Door

After leaving his cell, Bjorn utilized the ability to melt the metal door of the interrogation room, allowing him to proceed deeper into the facility.

### Destroying the Vampire Duke's Heart

In combat with a Vampire Duke, Bjorn repeatedly triggered Flesh Explosion against the vampire's chest, eventually destroying its heart and securing his victory.
