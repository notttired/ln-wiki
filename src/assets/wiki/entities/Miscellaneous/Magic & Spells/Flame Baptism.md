# Flame Baptism

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Spell |
| **Element** | Fire |
| **User** | Raven |
| **First Appearance** | Chapter 30 |

Flame Baptism is a fire-aligned offensive spell capable of incinerating multiple targets within a confined linear space. When cast, it generates a torrent of flames that fills a straight corridor, making it particularly effective against clustered enemies in tight quarters. The spell was notably employed by the mage Raven during the incursion into the Underground Prison, where it instantly annihilated a group of Banshees blocking the passageway. The intensity of the flames leaves no trace of the victims, suggesting a high degree of destructive power. As a fire spell, it likely draws upon elemental mana, but its precise incantation and mana cost remain undocumented.

## Properties

Flame Baptism is classified as a fire-elemental evocation spell. Its area of effect is a straight-line corridor, making it ideal for narrow spaces. The spell consumes the targets entirely, leaving no remains, indicating it deals fire damage with possible disintegration effects.

## Notable Uses

In Chapter 30, Raven used Flame Baptism to clear a corridor in the Underground Prison, killing all Banshees present in a single cast.
