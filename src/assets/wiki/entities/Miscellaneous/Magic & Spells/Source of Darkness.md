# Source of Darkness

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Passive Skill |
| **Source** | Vampire Guardian Essence |
| **Acquisition Chapter** | 35 |
| **Effect** | |
| Description | Prevents death until the heart is destroyed; user survives at 0% HP |

The Source of Darkness is a passive skill derived from the Vampire Guardian Essence. It fundamentally alters the survivability of its bearer by severing the link between typical mortal wounds and death. As long as the bearer's heart remains intact, they cannot die—even if their health points drop to zero. This makes the skill exceptionally dangerous in combat, as the user can continue fighting beyond the point where others would fall, requiring an opponent to specifically target the heart to achieve a permanent kill. The skill is acquired upon absorbing the Vampire Guardian Essence, a process that permanently binds the essence's power to the user. Bjorn obtained this skill in Chapter 35 after absorbing the essence, granting him a significant edge in survivability against enemies who are unaware of this unique weakness.

## Acquisition

Source of Darkness was obtained by Bjorn in Chapter 35 after he absorbed the Vampire Guardian Essence. The essence fused with his being, granting him the passive ability. This acquisition marks a turning point in his combat capabilities, allowing him to endure fatal blows that would otherwise kill any normal fighter.
