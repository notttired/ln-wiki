# Tarjin

Tarjin is a professional porter contracted by the mage Arua Raven to accompany her during an expedition into the Rift. A human man of sturdy build, he carries equipment and supplies through the hazardous terrain, fulfilling the essential logistical role of a porter. He is first seen in the company of Raven as they enter the Rift, maintaining a silent and watchful presence. After the party's battle with a Corpse Golem, Tarjin moves quickly to Raven's side and physically blocks the approach of Bjorn and Ainar, his body language and stern warning making clear his distrust of the two adventurers. He remains steadfastly by Raven's side, evidently prioritizing her safety over any potential alliance with the others.

## Personality

Tarjin demonstrates strong loyalty to his employer, Arua Raven, and a marked wariness of strangers. He does not hesitate to place himself between Raven and perceived threats, even when those threats are fellow party members. His actions in the aftermath of the Corpse Golem fight suggest a deep-seated distrust of outsiders and a fierce protective instinct.

## Relationships

Tarjin's interactions with others are primarily defined by his contractual duty and personal allegiance to Arua Raven.

### Arua Raven

Tarjin is employed by Raven as her porter for the Rift expedition. He remains loyally at her side throughout the journey and actively shields her from potential threats, including other adventurers.

### Bjorn and Ainar

Tarjin views Bjorn and Ainar with suspicion. After the Corpse Golem battle, he physically blocks their advance toward Raven and warns them to stay away, indicating a lack of trust in the pair.
