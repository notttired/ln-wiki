Ramod is a human explorer who served as a member of Darvan's group. He was one of four explorers who betrayed Bjorn and Ainar, siding with Darvan's treachery. During the confrontation, Ramod urged his companions to kill Bjorn without delay, showing a cruel and direct nature. However, his cruelty was short-lived; after Bjorn freed Ainar, Ramod was slain by Ainar's greatsword. His death marked the end of his involvement in the conflict, and little is known of his life prior to the betrayal.

## Personality

Ramod displayed a ruthless and impatient disposition. He advocated for a quick execution of Bjorn, suggesting a willingness to resort to violence without hesitation.

## Relationships

Ramod's interactions were limited to his fellow explorers and his targets.

### Darvan

The leader of the explorer group Ramod belonged to. Ramod followed Darvan's orders in the betrayal.

### Bjorn

A fellow explorer who was betrayed by Ramod's group. Ramod specifically urged to kill Bjorn quickly.

### Ainar

The explorer Ramod helped betray. After Ainar was freed by Bjorn, Ainar killed Ramod with a greatsword.

## Death

Ramod was killed by Ainar using a greatsword shortly after Ainar was freed by Bjorn. His death was swift, mirroring the quick end he had wished for Bjorn.
