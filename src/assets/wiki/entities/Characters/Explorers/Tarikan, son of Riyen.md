Tarikan, son of Riyen, is an Evil Spirit—a reincarnated being from another world—who was summoned into the body of a Barbarian tribesman. He arrived on the continent approximately one month before the summoning of Bjorn, another Evil Spirit. To survive the Barbarian Coming of Age Ceremony, Tarikan relied on being the last candidate called, thus avoiding detection as an imposter. However, his fear overwhelmed him during the ceremony, and he failed to enter the Labyrinth, a critical rite of passage for his new people.

Since that failure, Tarikan has been unable to integrate into Barbarian society. He sold the starting weapon given to him for fifty thousand Stone, a decision that left him defenseless and unable to hunt or earn his keep. For three months he has wandered the streets, homeless and starving, clad only in tattered, dirt-stained pants, with a shaggy beard marking his neglect. Without a weapon or the means to acquire one, he has been reduced to begging, a state that stands in stark contrast to the proud warrior tradition of the Barbarians.

In a desperate act, Tarikan approached Bjorn for assistance. Bjorn, recognizing him as a fellow Evil Spirit due to his lack of typical Barbarian pride and weapon, gave him one hundred fifty thousand Stone and warned him never to seek him again. Tarikan accepted the aid and departed, his future uncertain.

## Background

Tarikan was summoned into the body of a Barbarian approximately one month before Bjorn. He survived the Coming of Age Ceremony by being the last candidate called, thereby avoiding the scrutiny that would have exposed him. However, fear prevented him from entering the Labyrinth, the ceremony's crucial trial, leading to his failure to complete the rite.

## Personality and Behavior

Tarikan exhibits traits atypical of a Barbarian. He speaks with formal, stilted language and is easily startled, showing none of the pride or martial bearing characteristic of his adoptive people. Bjorn notes that his lack of a weapon and his beggarly demeanor mark him as an outsider, likely an Evil Spirit.

## Appearance

Tarikan is described as having a shaggy beard and wearing only tattered, dirt-stained pants. He appears utterly miserable, leaning against alley walls, and is unrecognizable as a Barbarian warrior.

## Relationships



### Bjorn

Bjorn, a fellow Evil Spirit, encounters Tarikan in destitution. Recognizing his plight, Bjorn gives him 150,000 Stone and orders him never to return. The encounter is transactional and final, with no ongoing bond.
