Riol Worb Dwalki, commonly known as Dwalki, is an Eighth Rank Mage who presents himself as a figure of considerable status. He claims recognition from the Rafdonia Royal Family and ties to the House of Baron Martoan, though the veracity of these affiliations remains uncertain. Dwalki is a genuine mage with a particular aptitude for lifestyle magic, curses, and Ice Magic, yet his credentials are often overshadowed by his grandiose self-presentation.

Dwalki possesses a proud and convoluted speech pattern, frequently introducing himself with elaborate titles that confuse those around him. His personality is marked by bluff and bluster, giving him the air of a con artist. Despite this, he is capable of genuine magical feats and follows orders without hesitation once he joins a team. He has little experience navigating the Labyrinth, a fact that does not deter his confident demeanor.

In Chapter 48, Dwalki applies to join the team of Bjorn and Hikurod, boasting of his noble connections and official Mage recognition. Shortly after joining in Chapter 49, he demonstrates his magic by casting a bright Light spell upon entering the Labyrinth, illuminating the darkness in a display that is effective but noted to be wasteful of mana. This act underscores both his competence and his lack of efficiency in practical adventuring.

## Background

Dwalki claims to be an Eighth Rank Mage recognized by the Rafdonia Royal Family and connected to the House of Baron Martoan. These credentials are unverified, and his actual background suggests a more humble origin. He presents himself with an air of authority, using his claimed titles to gain entry into adventuring parties.

## Personality

Dwalki is characterized by a bluffing and blustering demeanor, often speaking in a proud and convoluted manner that confuses others. He gives off the impression of a con artist, yet he is genuine in his magical abilities and follows orders once he commits to a group. His confidence can sometimes outpace his experience, especially in unfamiliar environments like the Labyrinth.

## Abilities

As an Eighth Rank Mage, Dwalki has demonstrated proficiency in several magical disciplines. He specializes in lifestyle magic, curses, and Ice Magic. He also knows a Light spell, which he used to illuminate the Labyrinth despite its high mana cost. However, he is inexperienced in the Labyrinth and may not yet be adept at managing his resources efficiently in combat or exploration.
