# Hans

| | |
|---|---|
| **Real Name** | Hans |
| **Occupation** | Explorer |
| **First Appearance** | Chapter 44 |

Hans is an Explorer active in the region where Bjorn travels. He frequents local inns and is familiar with the workings of the Adventurer's Guild. After noticing Bjorn alone following a companion's departure, Hans approaches him with practical advice. He recommends that Bjorn seek new party members through the Guild, emphasizing that the use of recording orbs has become a standard precaution against betrayal. Hans's friendly gesture of buying drinks and offering guidance underscores his helpful nature. His counsel plays a role in steering Bjorn toward the formal party recruitment process.

## Personality

Hans displays a sociable and generous disposition. He is not hesitant to approach a stranger and strike up a conversation, offering both companionship and practical wisdom. His willingness to cover the cost of drinks exemplifies his magnanimity, while his detailed knowledge of Guild policies reveals a seasoned Explorer who keeps abreast of changing norms.
