Erwen Fornachi di Tersia is a 20-year-old Fairy explorer from the Fornachi di Tersia tribe and the younger sister of Daria Witember di Tersia. She possesses pointy ears, amber cat-like eyes, and a tiny frame, standing approximately 1.6 meters tall. After a dangerous Labyrinth expedition alongside her sister, she sought out Bjorn at his inn, only to learn of his presumed death. This revelation sparked a desperate resolve to become stronger, leading her to train relentlessly. She now hears the voice of the fire, a sign of her deepening connection to spirit magic and her growing, almost malicious, determination.

## Background

Erwen belongs to the Fornachi di Tersia tribe and has a younger sibling living in the city. She first appears injured in a forest, where she is found by Bjorn. Initially fearful, she accepts his help and later becomes his Night Companion through a mutual oath. She accompanies him on the First Floor, demonstrating her archery and spirit magic skills. After a period of training with her sister Daria, she returns to find Bjorn missing and is told he is dead. She resolves to become stronger and begins intense training, often summoning spirits and communicating with fire.

## Personality

Erwen starts as extremely timid and wary, but after forming an oath with Bjorn, she becomes more curious and relaxed around him. She shows greed for money, agreeing to a 9:1 loot split without protest in order to earn for her younger brother. However, after believing Bjorn is dead, her personality darkens. She becomes desperate, determined, and even malicious, training with a single-minded intensity that worries her sister Daria.

## Abilities

Erwen has developed several abilities as an explorer, primarily focusing on archery and spirit magic.

### Fire Spirit Magic

Erwen can summon a flame spirit that provides light and can be used for combat. The flame can last up to ten hours.

### Wind Spirit Contract

After multiple attempts, Erwen successfully contracted a Wind Spirit using a Spirit Stone. The wind spirit allows her to alter the trajectory of her arrows or shoot faster.

### Bow Proficiency

Erwen is highly skilled with a bow, able to shoot enemies through the forehead at range and between the eyes at close range. She also recovers arrows after use.

### Trap Detection

Erwen demonstrates excellent trap detection, spotting hidden traps from a distance in darkness. She also scouts silently and determines enemy positions.

## Relationships



### Bjorn

Erwen's Night Companion and trusted partner. They swore a mutual oath, and she accompanied him on expeditions. She was devastated when she thought he was dead.

### Daria Witember di Tersia

Erwen's older sister, who trains her in the Labyrinth. Daria is concerned about Erwen's increasingly desperate training.

### Younger Sibling

Erwen has a younger sibling living in the city, whom she is motivated to support financially.
