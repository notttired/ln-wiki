Hikurod Murad is a Dwarf Explorer and team leader active in the Bloodstained Fortress Rift. He first appeared as a random explorer joining Bjorn and Ainar's expedition, where his pragmatic nature and combat prowess quickly became evident. Murad fought methodically with a hammer, crushing enemies from the bottom up, and negotiated hard to secure the valuable Numbers Item, the Guardian's Wristguard, for himself. His performance earned him a promotion to Sixth Rank after Bjorn suggested a rank review, which was approved within six days.

Murad possesses two notable Essence abilities: Counterweight, which grants knockback immunity and shock absorption, and Emergency Restoration, which restores equipment to its original state. He also demonstrated the ability to combine 'Lightning' and 'Discharge' to stun powerful foes, as seen during the battle against the Vampire Duke. Despite a temporary injury from a Corpse Golem attack that blinded him in one eye, Murad recovered and continued to fight effectively.

As a team leader, Murad has grown into a supportive and pragmatic figure. He takes care of eccentric members like Dwalki, respects team opinions, and learns from his experiences to become a better leader. His expedition team on the Third Floor benefits from his steady hand and experience.

## Background

Hikurod Murad began his career as a third-year Explorer in the Bloodstained Fortress Rift. He joined Bjorn and Ainar on an expedition that culminated in obtaining the Guardian's Wristguard (No. 3112), a Numbers Item valued at approximately 50 million Stone. After the successful expedition, Murad continued to operate as an explorer, eventually recruiting his own team for a Third Floor expedition. Bjorn applied to join, and Murad later promoted himself to Sixth Rank following Bjorn's suggestion.

## Personality

Murad is initially friendly but becomes confrontational when money is involved, as seen during loot negotiations. He is pragmatic and supportive as a leader, taking care of team members like Dwalki despite their eccentricities. He respects team opinions and continuously learns to improve his leadership. In battle, he shows determination and recovers quickly from setbacks, such as his injury from the Corpse Golem.

## Abilities and Equipment

Murad's combat style relies on a hammer, with which he methodically crushes enemies. He possesses two Essence abilities and a unique Numbers Item.

### Counterweight

An Essence ability that grants knockback immunity and shock absorption, allowing Murad to hold the line against powerful opponents like the Vampire Duke.

### Emergency Restoration

An Essence ability that restores equipment to its original state, useful for maintaining gear durability during long engagements.

### Guardian's Wristguard (No. 3112)

A Numbers Item obtained from the Bloodstained Fortress, valued at 50 million Stone. It enhances Murad's defensive capabilities and is a testament to his successful expedition with Bjorn and Ainar.

## Relationships

Murad's interactions with key individuals shape his career and leadership style.

### Bjorn

Murad first met Bjorn during the Bloodstained Fortress expedition, where they fought together against the Corpse Golem and Vampire Duke. Bjorn later joined Murad's expedition team and suggested that Murad apply for a rank promotion, which was successful.

### Raven

Raven was part of the loot negotiation in the Bloodstained Fortress, working alongside Murad and Bjorn to distribute rewards.

### Dwalki

Murad leads a team that includes Dwalki, whose eccentric personality Murad manages with patience and support, fostering a cohesive group dynamic.

### Ainar

Ainar fought alongside Murad in the Bloodstained Fortress, contributing to the successful defeat of the Vampire Duke.
