Arua Raven is a mage of the Sixth Rank, affiliated with the Mage Tower and a member of the Altemion School. Dispatched to support the Guild branch, she joined Bjorn's party for the Rift expedition, quickly asserting herself as a capable leader. Her passion for magic and research is matched only by her pride, and she does not hesitate to show off her abilities or demand recognition. Despite her combat prowess, she has a notable vulnerability to strong odors—a weakness that nearly incapacitated her in the Corpse Golem's lair. During the expedition through the Bloodstained Fortress and subsequent battles, Raven demonstrated a versatile arsenal of spells, including area-of-effect attacks, curse dispelling, and the powerful Black Sun Orb. After the defeat of the Vampire Duke, she took possession of the Necronomicon and the Rift Stone, securing valuable resources for her research. Her sharp mind for negotiation came to the fore when she blackmailed the Regional Chief into paying a 1 million Stones service fee for her magical documentation of an interrogation. Back in the city, Raven proposed exclusive research on Bjorn's Vampire Essence, bargaining for a share of the compensation. In her laboratory, she examined the essence and identified four skills inherent to it, but her attention soon turned to the mysterious Tear of the Goddess. When her master Deian showed unusual interest in Bjorn, Raven grew uneasy. She has since become determined to uncover the secrets hidden within Bjorn's essence, suspecting that the Tear of the Goddess may be involved.

## Background

Arua Raven is a member of the Mage Tower and the Altemion School. She was dispatched by the Tower to provide support for the Guild branch, a task that allowed her to join the Rift expedition and demonstrate her abilities.

## Personality

Raven is proud and loves to show off her magical prowess. She is passionate about magic and research and responds positively to admiration. However, she has a notable weakness: strong odors. Though she is accustomed to gory sights from her anatomical studies, the stench of the Corpse Golem's lair caused her to vomit repeatedly, nearly incapacitating her.

## Abilities and Magic

Arua Raven is a Sixth-Rank mage with a diverse repertoire of spells, including offensive, support, and enhancement magic.

### Blade Storm

An area-of-effect wind magic that conjures blades of wind to strike multiple foes.

### Curse Dispelling

A spell that removes curses and negative magical effects from allies.

### Wound Aggravation

A spell that aggravates existing injuries, causing further harm to the target.

### Flash Explosion

A blinding flash spell used to disorient enemies.

### Black Sun Orb

A powerful Sixth-Rank sun-attribute attack that deals massive damage. It requires several minutes of preparation and can be enhanced with Attribute Enhancement.

### Attribute Enhancement

An enhancement spell used to amplify the power of other spells, particularly effective with Black Sun Orb.

## Notable Exploits

Arua Raven led the party through the Bloodstained Fortress, negotiated loot distribution, and discovered the hidden Necronomicon. She used Flash Explosion to blind the vampire and later cast the devastating Black Sun Orb. After the expedition, she blackmailed the Regional Chief for a 1 million Stones fee and proposed exclusive research on Bjorn's Vampire Essence. In her lab, she identified four skills in the essence and began investigating the Tear of the Goddess.
