Serdin is a member of the Crystal Alliance, an organization dedicated to hunting and eliminating those who threaten their interests. He was assigned to track the movements of Bjorn and Erwen, two individuals who had drawn the Alliance's attention. Serdin's mission was to monitor their activities and report back to his superiors, but his efforts were cut short when he was discovered by Erwen. In a confrontation in chapter 10.0, Erwen shot him through the forehead with an arrow as he attempted to use a Message Stone to alert his allies, killing him instantly.

## Death

Serdin met his end at the hands of Erwen in chapter 10.0. While trying to communicate via a Message Stone, he was shot through the forehead by Erwen's arrow, preventing him from alerting the Crystal Alliance to their location.
