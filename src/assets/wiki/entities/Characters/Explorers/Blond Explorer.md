# Blond Explorer

| | |
|---|---|

| **Race** | Human |
| **Height** | 160 cm |

The Blond Explorer is a human wanderer of unremarkable origin, recognized by his small stature and distinctive blond hair. Standing approximately 160 centimeters tall, he possesses a rare Gnome's Supernatural Ability that projects a zone of stealth within a 3-meter radius on the ground. This area stealth effect lasts for up to 30 minutes, extendable to 40 minutes, but the user must remain stationary while it is active.

Despite his unassuming appearance, the explorer's ability makes him a valuable asset in evading pursuit. His most documented encounter occurred when he was captured by Bjorn, a formidable raider. Under duress, the Blond Explorer was forced to activate his stealth field to allow Bjorn and his group to escape detection from a red-haired raider. The incident highlights both the utility of his power and the peril he faces as a non-combatant in a harsh world.

Little else is known about the Blond Explorer's past or motivations. His current whereabouts remain uncertain, and his fate is intertwined with the actions of those who would exploit his unique gift.

## Abilities

The Blond Explorer's supernatural power is derived from a Gnome's Supernatural Ability, granting him a unique area stealth effect.

### Area Stealth

An ability that creates a zone of invisibility within a 3-meter radius on the ground, concealing anyone inside. The user cannot move while the ability is active.
