# Hartz Young

| | |
|---|---|
| **Affiliation** | Crystal Alliance |
| **Vital Status** | Executed |

Hartz Young is a former member of the Crystal Alliance clan. He gained notoriety for providing false testimony against Bjorn Yandel, which directly led to Bjorn being sentenced to death. Following the exposure of his perjury during a trial, Hartz was himself condemned to execution at the Execution Ground. Prior to his downfall, Hartz also placed a bounty of 20,000 Stone on Erwen, offering the reward and the privilege of having her first to the captor. Bjorn Yandel, having survived the death sentence, tracked Hartz down, beat him, and imprisoned him in a wardrobe. Hartz's false testimony ultimately sealed his own fate.
Hartz's actions reflect the corruption and vendettas within the Crystal Alliance. His scheme against Bjorn Yandel was driven by personal motives; however, the full extent of his reasoning remains unknown. After his arrest and imprisonment, the Regional Chief of the Execution Ground personally ordered his execution, marking a swift end to his life.

## Notable Incidents

Hartz Young is remembered for two major incidents: placing a bounty on Erwen and his false testimony against Bjorn Yandel.

### Bounty on Erwen

After being injured, Hartz raised the bounty on Erwen to 20,000 Stone, promising the captor the reward and the opportunity to have Erwen first.
