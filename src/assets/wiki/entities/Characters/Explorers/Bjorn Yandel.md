Bjorn Yandel is a Barbarian Explorer and the son of Yandel, a name that carries weight among the Barbarian tribes. Originally a player of the game Dungeon & Stone, he was transported into its reality after death, retaining his knowledge of game mechanics. He entered the Labyrinth through a dimensional instability exploit, starting his journey on the First Floor with a cunning that defies the typical Barbarian stereotype. Through quick thinking and ruthless pragmatism, he survived betrayals, forged alliances, and steadily grew stronger.

Within a short span, Bjorn underwent the Immortal Engraving Stage 1, which greatly increased his regeneration and physical stats. He absorbed two powerful essences: the Rank 7 Corpse Golem Essence and the Rank 5 Vampire Guardian Essence, gaining a suite of skills including Source of Darkness, which prevents death so long as his heart is intact, and Immortal One, which boosts regeneration proportionally to lost health. These abilities allowed him to survive a fatal neck wound and defeat the Vampire Duke Cambormir in the Bloodstained Fortress Rift.

After the Vampire Duke battle, Bjorn was unjustly arrested by the Explorer's Guild on false charges of raiding. Sentenced to death, he orchestrated a daring prison break, took the Regional Chief's daughter hostage, and forced a public trial. With the aid of the mage Arua Raven, he proved his innocence through recording magic, exposing Guild corruption. He was released, promoted to Rank 7, and awarded 5 million Stones in compensation. He later negotiated additional compensation from the Mage Tower, acquiring the Frost Spirit Ring.

Now a wealthy and influential explorer, Bjorn uses his resources to purchase high-tier equipment, including a Lithium Alloy breastplate and shield, and seeks to form a stable expedition team. He remains driven by the hope of returning to his original world through the Gate of the Abyss, though setbacks have forced him to consider a permanent life in this reality. His experiences have blended his original personality with Barbarian instincts, making him more aggressive and straightforward, yet his strategic mind and adaptability continue to set him apart.

## Background

Bjorn was a player of the virtual reality game Dungeon & Stone who died and reincarnated into the game world as a Barbarian. He retained his meta-knowledge of the game's systems, including the satiety mechanic, Night Companions, and dimensional instability. After a month of training, he entered the Labyrinth through the Dimensional Plaza, exploiting a portal close-time to skip the First Floor and directly access the Second Floor. This knowledge gave him a significant advantage over native explorers.

## Personality

Bjorn presents himself as a typical brutish Barbarian but is highly intelligent and calculating. He uses the stereotypes of his race to lower others' guards, feigning simplicity to hide his true intentions. He is ruthless when necessary, killing in self-defense without hesitation, but also shows loyalty to those he trusts. His time in the Barbarian body has increased his aggressive instincts, leading to a fusion of his original cautious nature with the Barbarian's straightforward combativeness. He is driven by a desire to return to his original world, but pragmatically adapts to circumstances.

## Abilities

Bjorn's power stems from his Immortal Engraving and absorbed essences, granting him extreme durability and regneration.

### Essences

Essences absorbed into Bjorn's soul, granting permanent stat boosts and skills.

#### Corpse Golem Essence (Rank 7)

Absorbed during the fight against the Vampire Duke. Grants Pain Resistance +70, Bone Density +55, Strength +15, and the skills Acidic Fluid and Flesh Explosion.

#### Vampire Guardian Essence (Rank 5)

Obtained after defeating Vampire Duke Cambormir. Grants Source of Darkness (cannot die while heart is intact) and Immortal One (regeneration increases with damage taken). Also provides stat boosts to Strength, Agility, and Magic Resistance.

### Skills

Active and passive abilities derived from essences and training.

#### Source of Darkness (Passive)

Prevents death as long as the heart remains intact. A core survival skill from the Vampire Guardian Essence.

#### Sacrificial Mark (Active)

Temporarily increases physical abilities based on marked living creatures. Gained from the Vampire Guardian Essence.

#### Immortal One (Passive)

Greatly increases regeneration in proportion to lost health. Synergizes with the Immortal Engraving.

#### Blood Master (Active)

Absorbs one target skill upon direct vampirism. From the Vampire Guardian Essence.

#### Flesh Explosion (Active)

Detonates own flesh to deal damage or break objects. Learned from Corpse Golem Essence. Used to destroy the Vampire Duke's heart and break prison bars.

## Equipment

Notable items owned by Bjorn, many purchased after his compensation.

### Lithium Alloy Breastplate

White, light, and hard armor purchased for 1,910,000 Stone. Designed to protect the heart, synergizing with Source of Darkness.

### Lithium Alloy Battle Shield

A durable shield costing 2,100,000 Stone, allowing Bjorn to serve as a frontline protector.

### Expansion Bag

A magical bag purchased for 2,500,000 Stone. Enchanted with Auto-Sort, Individual Withdrawal, and Lighten (Low). Greatly increases inventory capacity.

### Frost Spirit Ring

A ring obtained as additional compensation from the Mage Tower. Its full capabilities are known only to Bjorn and Deian Tveherion.

### Golden Mask

A mask from the Bloodstained Fortress, valued at 500,000 Stone. Bjorn keeps it for potential use in disguise.

## Relationships

Key individuals in Bjorn's life, ranging from allies to enemies.

### Erwen

A fairy archer and close companion. Bjorn saved her from the Crystal Alliance and formed a temporary oath. She respects him deeply and seeks to become stronger to aid him.

### Ainar

A Barbarian woman who became Bjorn's first formal party member. They sparred and fought together in the Bloodstained Fortress. She stayed by his side while he was unconscious and later left to train at the Holy Land.

### Arua Raven

A mage from the Altemion School. Initially an ally in the Bloodstained Fortress, she later helped Bjorn escape the Guild's false charges by providing recording magic. Their relationship is transactional but cordial, with mutual suspicion.

### Hikurod Murad

A dwarf explorer and leader of a team Bjorn recently joined. They met during the Bloodstained Fortress expedition and have mutual respect. Hikurod values Bjorn's advice and treats him as a trusted teammate.
