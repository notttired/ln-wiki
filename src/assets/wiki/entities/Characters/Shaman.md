# Shaman

| | |
|---|---|
| **Occupation** | Shaman |
| **Affiliation** | Barbarian Tribe |
| **Status** | Alive |

The Shaman is a blindfolded figure belonging to a Barbarian tribe, entrusted with the sacred duty of performing the Immortal Engraving ritual on warriors. During the procedure, he uses needles to trace the subject's tattoos, a process that is reportedly painful. When performing the ritual on Bjorn, the Shaman observed that Bjorn possessed unusually clean soul circuits, noting that maintaining this purity would enable spirits to grant power. The Shaman's blindfold suggests a focus on spiritual perception rather than physical sight, and his role is central to the empowerment of the tribe's warriors through the engraving rite.

## Appearance

The Shaman is always seen wearing a blindfold, concealing his eyes. He carries needles that he uses to perform the Immortal Engraving. Bjorn found the procedure to be painful.

## Role

The Shaman performs the Immortal Engraving ritual on barbarian warriors. He comments on the state of the subject's soul circuits, noting that clean circuits allow spirits to grant power. His work is essential for warriors to gain the blessings of spirits.
