# Tribe Chief

| | |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Tribe Chief |

The Tribe Chief is the leader of the Barbarian tribe that resides in the Holy Land. He is a powerful and respected figure, known for his mastery of Spirit Engravings—a sacred practice that enhances the abilities of warriors. As the chief, he personally greets visitors to the Holy Land and evaluates their worthiness for the engraving rituals. His demeanor is intimidating, often causing even seasoned warriors like Bjorn to feel wary in his presence.

Beneath his fearsome exterior, the Tribe Chief possesses a fiery yet generous personality. He has a particular appreciation for cleverness and wit, as demonstrated when Bjorn recounted his tale of tricking the fairy Erwen. The chief laughed heartily at the story and rewarded Bjorn with a substantial discount of 200,000 Stones for an Immortal Engraving, a gesture that highlights his magnanimity toward those who earn his favor.

Though initially perceived as harsh, the chief's actions reveal a leader who values strength of character and cunning alongside combat prowess. His role as the guardian of the Holy Land's engraving tradition makes him a pivotal figure for any warrior seeking to enhance their power.

## Background

The Tribe Chief serves as the chieftain of the Barbarian tribe inhabiting the Holy Land. His primary responsibility is overseeing the performance of Spirit Engravings, a sacred rite that imbues warriors with enhanced capabilities. He is the gatekeeper to this tradition, personally evaluating those who seek its benefits.

## Personality

The Tribe Chief is characterized by a fiery temperament and a generous spirit. Despite his intimidating presence, he possesses a jovial side, especially when presented with tales of cleverness and trickery. His reaction to Bjorn's story about fleecing the fairy Erwen—laughing heartily and granting a large discount—exemplifies his appreciation for wit and his willingness to reward those who amuse him. Warriors who initially fear him may find themselves warming to his larger-than-life personality.

## Abilities

The Tribe Chief is a skilled practitioner of Spirit Engravings. He personally performs the engravings, a process that grants warriors significant enhancements. His expertise allows him to offer discounts to promising individuals, as seen with Bjorn, indicating his ability to assess a warrior's potential and adjust his services accordingly.

## Relationships

The Tribe Chief has a notable interaction with the warrior Bjorn. Upon hearing Bjorn's tale of tricking a fairy, the chief was greatly amused and awarded him a 200,000 Stone discount on an Immortal Engraving. This interaction showcases the chief's respect for cunning and his generous nature.
