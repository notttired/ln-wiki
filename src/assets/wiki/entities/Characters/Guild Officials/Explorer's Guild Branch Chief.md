The Branch Chief of the Rafdonia Explorer's Guild is a former explorer who now oversees the guild's operations in Rafdonia. He holds a position of authority within the guild hierarchy, responsible for managing local Explorer activities. His past as an explorer gives him practical experience, though his current role is primarily administrative.

In recent events, the Branch Chief was subdued by the escaped prisoner Bjorn, who used the Regional Chief's daughter as a hostage to force compliance. Demonstrating strategic pragmatism, the Branch Chief complied with Bjorn's demands to prevent further harm. He called off the guards and summoned specific individuals as instructed, including the Investigator Hartz Young and a Mage.

Currently, the Branch Chief is acting under duress, forced to cooperate with Bjorn's plan for a public trial. His actions are shaped by the immediate threat to the Regional Chief's daughter and his desire to minimize casualties. Despite his compromised position, he exercises his authority to carry out Bjorn's orders, maintaining a careful balance between duty and coercion.

## Background

The Branch Chief was once an active explorer before ascending to an administrative role. His experience in the field likely informs his current decisions, though his primary responsibilities now involve oversight and coordination of guild activities in Rafdonia.

## Personality

The Branch Chief displays a pragmatic approach to crisis situations. When confronted by Bjorn with a hostage, he quickly assessed the situation and chose compliance over confrontation to protect innocent lives. This strategic compliance indicates a priority on minimizing harm rather than upholding protocol at all costs.
