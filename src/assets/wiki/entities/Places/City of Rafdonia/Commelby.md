# Commelby

| | |
|---|---|
| **Also Known As** | Commercial District |
| **Location** | Zones 2 to 5, Imperial Capital Karnon |
| **Type** | Free Market District |

Commelby, also known as the Commercial District, is the free market area surrounding the Imperial Capital Karnon. It occupies Zones 2 through 5 of the city, forming the most commercially active region in the capital. The district is home to the middle class, artisans, and a dense concentration of shops, market stalls, and trading posts. Its streets are perpetually crowded and bustling with merchants, customers, and travelers conducting business.

The Commercial District serves as the primary economic hub of Karnon, where goods from across the empire change hands. Adventurers and locals alike frequent Commelby to sell loot, purchase equipment, and procure supplies. For instance, the paladin Bjorn visited Commelby alongside his companion Ainar to sell equipment and acquire new items, reflecting the district's role as a vital commercial thoroughfare.

## Layout

Commelby spans Zones 2 to 5 of Karnon, encircling the inner districts. The area is a layered marketplace with streets lined with shops and open-air stalls. The district's organization facilitates a steady flow of traffic, though crowding is common.

## Economic Activity

As the city's free market, Commelby hosts a wide variety of trades. Artisans and merchants operate permanent establishments alongside temporary vendors. Equipment dealers, item shops, and equipment exchanges are prevalent, catering to both civilians and adventurers. The district is known for its competitive prices and diverse inventory.

## Notable Visits

In a notable instance, the paladin Bjorn and his companion Ainar visited Commelby to sell unwanted equipment and purchase new gear. Their visit underscores the district's function as a destination for both liquidating assets and procuring necessities.
