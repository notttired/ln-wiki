The Dimensional Plaza is a large open square located in Rafdonia, serving as the primary gathering point for explorers seeking entry into the Labyrinth. Each month at midnight, a shimmering orb of light manifests at the plaza's central point, forming a portal to the Labyrinth. Thousands of explorers crowd into the plaza, waiting for the official signal before rushing through the portal. The portal gradually shrinks over time, forcing latecomers to enter before it closes entirely. The plaza is typically empty except during these monthly events, when it becomes a hub of activity and anticipation.

## Portal Manifestation

The portal opens precisely at midnight once per month, appearing as a brilliant orb of light that hovers at the plaza's center. It remains open for a limited time, slowly shrinking as explorers pass through. Official signals coordinate the entry process, and crowds often thin out before the portal closes, with only the most daring or delayed explorers slipping through at the last moment.
