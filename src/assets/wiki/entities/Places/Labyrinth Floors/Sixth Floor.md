# Sixth Floor

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Floor |
| **Part of** | Labyrinth |
| **First Appearance** | Chapter 18 |

The Sixth Floor is a level of the Labyrinth, a dangerous underground dungeon known for its rewards and perils. For Bjorn, the Sixth Floor holds particular significance as a financial milestone. Through careful calculation, he determines that reaching at least this floor would provide enough wealth to live comfortably until retirement. As a result, the Sixth Floor becomes a concrete intermediate goal, representing a threshold of financial security that drives his early expeditions. While the floor itself is not further described, its importance lies in its role as a benchmark for success in Bjorn's personal ambitions.

## Financial Milestone

Bjorn uses the Sixth Floor as a specific target to ensure a comfortable retirement. He calculates that the rewards obtained by reaching this floor will be sufficient to sustain him financially for the rest of his life. This makes the Sixth Floor a clear and measurable objective, distinct from the broader goal of simply advancing through the Labyrinth.
