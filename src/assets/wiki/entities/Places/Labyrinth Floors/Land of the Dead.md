The Land of the Dead is a perilous region located on the Second Floor of the Labyrinth. It is a barren expanse of black earth dotted with the ruins of ancient stone buildings, giving it a desolate and foreboding appearance. An eerie silence hangs in the air, occasionally broken by faint mournful cries that echo across the landscape, heightening the sense of dread that permeates the area. This nightmarish realm is exclusively inhabited by undead creatures, ranging from lowly Ghouls and Skeletons to more terrifying entities such as Banshees and Death Fiends. The deeper reaches of the Land of the Dead are known to host higher-species monsters of the Eighth Rank, making it increasingly dangerous the further one ventures. Adventurers who dare to hunt here must be prepared to face relentless hordes of the undead, as the region offers little respite. Among those who have braved its horrors are Bjorn and Ainar, who once conducted a hunt within its borders, encountering a Death Fiend and other undead abominations.

## Geography

The Land of the Dead is characterized by black earth and ruins of stone buildings. An eerie atmosphere permeates the region, with faint mournful cries carried on the air.

## Inhabitants

The region is populated by a variety of undead monsters, including Ghouls, Elder Ghouls, Skeletons, Banshees, Dullahan, and Death Fiends. Deeper areas host higher-species monsters of the Eighth Rank.

## Notable Events

Bjorn and Ainar once hunted in the Land of the Dead, encountering Banshees, Skeletons, and a powerful Death Fiend.
