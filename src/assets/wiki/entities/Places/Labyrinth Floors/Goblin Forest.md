The Goblin Forest is a treacherous area on the Second Floor, serving as the first environment encountered after activating the portal on the First Floor. It is a dark forest with a high ceiling that resembles an artificial sky, dotted with star-like lights that provide only dim illumination, comparable to an unlit alleyway. This ceiled sky does not brighten over time, maintaining perpetual twilight. The trees within the forest are unnaturally tough, resisting attempts to cut or break them; a hammer strike leaves no mark. The forest floor is riddled with hidden traps, making every step dangerous. The area is also home to numerous goblin groups, adding to the threat. The combination of poor visibility, resilient vegetation, and concealed dangers makes the Goblin Forest a challenging environment for any explorer.

## Appearance

The Goblin Forest is perpetually shrouded in dim light from star-like points on a high ceiling that forms the 'sky'. This ceiling does not change brightness over time, maintaining constant twilight. The trees are extremely tough, resistant to physical attacks such as hammer strikes, and cannot be knocked down.

## Hazards

The ground is covered with numerous traps hidden among the undergrowth, posing constant risk to anyone traversing the forest. Additionally, goblin groups patrol the area, ready to ambush intruders.
