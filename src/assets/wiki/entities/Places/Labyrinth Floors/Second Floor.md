The Second Floor of the Labyrinth is a treacherous dark forest accessible only through portals descending from the First Floor. Upon arrival, adventurers find themselves in an area known as the Goblin Forest, where the ceiling is so high that it appears as a starry sky, casting dim light comparable to an unlit alleyway. The forest's trees are remarkably resilient, capable of withstanding heavy hammer blows without falling. The ground is riddled with hidden traps, requiring cautious navigation. Monsters on this floor congregate in larger packs of ten or more, including more dangerous variants such as Mutants and higher-ranked creatures, making survival a constant challenge. The floor's unique ecosystem and heightened dangers make it a significant step up in difficulty from the First Floor. Adventurers seeking greater rewards often venture here despite the risks, as the concentration of monsters offers increased opportunities for hunting. The perpetual twilight created by the false sky never changes, disorienting those accustomed to natural cycles. The tough trees also serve as natural obstacles, preventing easy passage and forcing travelers to find clear paths through the dense undergrowth. Notably, the Second Floor was the destination chosen by Bjorn and Erwen after evading pursuit, hoping to leverage the floor's perils to lose their trail and increase their earnings.

## Appearance

The Second Floor is a vast dark forest with a high ceiling that mimics a starry sky. The stars are actually tiny lights that provide dim visibility, similar to an alleyway without streetlights. The ceiling does not brighten with time, maintaining a constant twilight. The trees are extremely tough, resisting attempts to chop them down, and the ground is uneven with many hidden traps.

## Hazards

Monsters on the Second Floor appear in larger groups, often ten or more, and include more dangerous types such as Mutants and higher-rank creatures. The forest is also filled with numerous traps, making navigation treacherous. The combination of hostile creatures and environmental hazards requires adventurers to proceed with extreme caution.

## Notable Visits

In one recorded expedition, the adventurers Bjorn and Erwen chose to descend to the Second Floor to evade pursuit and increase their earnings. Their arrival involved navigating through a portal and immediately facing the floor's challenges, including navigating through traps and avoiding monster packs.
