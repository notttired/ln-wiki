# Exchange Office for Ninth-Rank Explorers

| | |
|---|---|

The Exchange Office for Ninth-Rank Explorers is a government-operated facility located in Rafdonia. It serves as a vital resource for newly ranked adventurers, providing a safe and regulated environment to convert Magic Stones into currency. The office is a nondescript building, its interior designed for efficient transactions, with a counter separating the official from the explorers.

Staffed by a single public official, the office maintains a quiet atmosphere. However, beneath the counter lies a hidden emergency button, a discreet security measure that allows the official to summon guards from the nearby precinct if they sense anything amiss. This system underscores the government's oversight of the explorer profession, even at its lowest ranks.

In a notable incident, Bjorn, a Ninth-Rank Explorer, visited the office to exchange Magic Stones. The official became suspicious of him for reasons unknown and activated the emergency button, resulting in Bjorn's arrest by the summoned guards.

## Security Measures

The office is equipped with a hidden emergency button located under the counter. The official can press it to instantly summon guards from the nearby precinct in case of suspicious activity. This system was activated during Bjorn's visit, leading to his arrest.
