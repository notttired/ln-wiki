# Outermost Area

| | |
|---|---|

The Outermost Area is a treacherous region located within the enigmatic Labyrinth. Known for its extreme danger, it is a place where explorers rarely venture and even more rarely survive. The area is shrouded in mystery, with most knowledge of it coming from scarce observation records. These records describe it as a rare occurrence, suggesting that the Outermost Area is not a fixed location but rather an anomalous zone that appears under specific, poorly understood conditions.

One of the few documented encounters with the Outermost Area was experienced by the explorer Bjorn. His account, preserved in the observation records, provides a glimpse into the region's perilous nature. The details of his experience remain obscure, but it is clear that the Outermost Area poses a significant threat to anyone who finds themselves within its bounds. The rarity of such encounters underscores the area's elusiveness and the formidable challenges it presents to those seeking to explore the depths of the Labyrinth.

## Background

The Outermost Area is a dangerous and rarely encountered region within the Labyrinth. It is primarily known through observation records, which classify it as an uncommon phenomenon. The area's exact nature and location are poorly understood, adding to its reputation as a perilous anomaly.
