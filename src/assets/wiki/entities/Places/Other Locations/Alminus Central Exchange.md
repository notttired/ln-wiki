# Alminus Central Exchange

The Alminus Central Exchange is the primary marketplace in the commercial city of Commelby, located within Zone 2. It operates on a consignment model, allowing buyers to submit search criteria for desired items and pay a fee per successful acquisition. The exchange serves as a vital hub for adventurers and merchants seeking gear and supplies without the need for direct haggling.

Notable for its systematic and efficient process, the exchange attracted the attention of the adventurer Bjorn, who utilized its services to equip himself. His first visit resulted in the purchase of an Expansion Bag, and he later returned to acquire a suit of armor and a shield. These transactions underscore the exchange's role in supporting those who venture into the perilous zones beyond Commelby.

## Services

The Alminus Central Exchange uses a consignment sale system. Sellers list items, and buyers specify search criteria; the exchange then matches requests and charges a fee per item obtained. This method streamlines trade and ensures a wide variety of goods are available.

## Notable Transactions

During his preparation for expeditions, the adventurer Bjorn conducted several purchases at the exchange. His acquisitions included an Expansion Bag, a piece of enchanted storage equipment, followed by armor and a shield to enhance his combat capabilities.
