The Monument to the Great Sage is a stone tablet situated at the center of the First Floor of the Tower. Engraved with an inscription honoring the Last Great Sage, Difflun Groundel Gabrielius, the monument serves a vital function for those seeking to traverse between realms. Its surface bears no visible mechanisms, yet it responds to specific offerings of Magic Stones. When a Goblin, Gnome, Ghoul, and Blade Wolf Magic Stone are presented alongside an Eighth-Rank Magic Stone, the tablet absorbs them and a Rift portal opens, allowing passage to another location. The monument's purpose is both a tribute and a practical gateway, though its use is not without cost.

## Function

The monument can open a Rift when it receives an offering consisting of one Magic Stone each from a Goblin, Gnome, Ghoul, and Blade Wolf, plus a single Eighth-Rank Magic Stone. Upon offering, the stones vanish and a portal forms, providing access to a different area. This ritual was demonstrated by Bjorn in a documented instance, where he forcibly activated the monument to escape or reach a destination.
