The Barbarian Inn is a well-known establishment in the city of Rafdonia, serving as a gathering place exclusively for Barbarians. Its doors are closed to all other races, offering a haven where Barbarians can converse freely and share news of their far-flung kin. The inn is renowned for its rough-hewn architecture and hearty fare, reflecting the rugged traditions of its patrons. It serves as both a lodging and a hub for information, where travelers and locals alike exchange tales of battles, journeys, and the shifting politics of the Barbarian tribes.

## History

The inn was the site of a notable visit by Bjorn, a warrior seeking Ainar. Upon arriving, Bjorn discovered that Ainar had already departed for the Holy Land. While there, he also overheard rumors of a figure known as the "Barbarian of Freedom," a mysterious individual whose exploits were the subject of hushed conversations among the inn's patrons.

## Exclusivity

The Barbarian Inn strictly limits its clientele to Barbarians only. This policy ensures a safe and comfortable environment for Barbarians to conduct business, share information, and enjoy the camaraderie of their own kind. Non-Barbarians are turned away at the door, and the inn's reputation for exclusivity is fiercely upheld by its staff and regulars.
