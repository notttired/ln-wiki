The Execution Ground is a designated location within the realm where condemned criminals are put to death. It serves as the final destination for those who have been sentenced by the legal authorities, embodying the ultimate penalty for serious offenses. Though its exact whereabouts are not widely documented, its function is well understood as a place of grim finality. The Execution Ground gained notable mention following the sentencing of Hartz Young, whose fate was sealed to meet his end there after being found guilty of his crimes. The ground stands as a somber reminder of the justice system's capacity for capital punishment.

## Background

The Execution Ground is the site where capital sentences are carried out. It is referenced in legal proceedings as the destined location for convicted criminals like Hartz Young, who was sentenced to execution after his trial. The ground itself is not described in detail, but its purpose is clear within the judicial framework of the land.
