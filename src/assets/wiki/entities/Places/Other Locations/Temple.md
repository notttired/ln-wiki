# Temple

| | |
|---|---|
| **Location** | Rafdonia |
| **Type** | Religious institution |
| **Notable Demand** | Verification of innocence by Bjorn |

The Temple is a religious institution in Rafdonia, serving as a central authority in both spiritual and legal matters. Its priests are recognized as official arbiters who can validate claims of innocence and mediate disputes, reflecting the Temple's deep integration into the region's societal fabric. The Temple's teachings and rituals form the cornerstone of local faith, and its clergy hold significant sway over the daily lives of Rafdonia's inhabitants. In a notable incident, Bjorn demanded that an official priest from the Temple be brought to verify his innocence, underscoring the institution's trusted role as a bridge between divine judgment and earthly justice.

## Notable Events

In recorded accounts from the 38th chapter, Bjorn demanded that an official priest from the Temple be brought to verify his innocence. This event highlights the Temple's authority in legal and religious proceedings within Rafdonia.
