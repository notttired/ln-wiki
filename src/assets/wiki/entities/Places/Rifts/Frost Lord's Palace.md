# Frost Lord's Palace

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Rift |
| **Floor** | Eighth |
| **Difficulty** | Extremely Challenging |

The Frost Lord's Palace is a notorious Rift located on the Eighth Floor of the Tower. It is widely regarded as one of the most challenging Rifts on that floor, known to test even seasoned climbers to their limits. The palace is rumored to be an icy fortress guarded by powerful frost-based entities, though few details have been confirmed by reliable sources. Its name alone evokes caution among those who hear it, as many tales speak of adventurers who entered and never returned. The Rift's reputation has spread through word of mouth, making it a cautionary legend among Tower climbers.

## Background

The Frost Lord's Palace is mentioned by Ainar, a guide from the early floors, who recounts hearing stories about it from her own travels. She describes it as a place of extreme danger, emphasizing that even experienced parties avoid it unless they are well-prepared. The Rift's exact location on the Eighth Floor is known only through fragmented accounts, and its internal structure remains largely unexplored by the broader climbing community. The palace serves as a benchmark for difficulty, often referenced when comparing other hazardous zones.
