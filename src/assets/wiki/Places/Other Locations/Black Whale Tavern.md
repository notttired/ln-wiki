The Black Whale Tavern was a well-known establishment in the city of Rafdonia. For years, it served as a popular gathering place for locals and travelers alike. The tavern was chosen by the adventurers Bjorn and Erwen as their meeting point after they separated, indicating its familiarity and central location within the city.

However, the Black Whale Tavern closed its doors over a decade ago. The establishment changed hands and was renamed Paidenfuss, a name that remains in use to this day. The closure and renaming were precipitated by the original owner's son, who fell into gambling and ultimately caused the change in ownership and name. When Bjorn returned to Rafdonia and sought out the Black Whale, he learned from a local resident that the tavern no longer existed under its original name, having been replaced by Paidenfuss.

## History

The Black Whale Tavern operated in Rafdonia as a common meeting spot. It was specifically chosen by Bjorn and Erwen as the rendezvous point for their return. The tavern closed over ten years ago due to the original owner's son's gambling debt, leading to a rebranding as Paidenfuss.
