The Goblin District is a region on the First Floor of the Labyrinth, notorious for its dense population of goblin monsters. The area consists of winding tunnels and refuse-strewn chambers, often rigged with crude traps. Despite its dangers, the district holds strategic value as an assembly point for the Crystal Alliance during coordinated expeditions. Adventurers such as Bjorn have been known to traverse the district extensively, navigating its treacherous passages while hunting goblins.

## Geography

The Goblin District occupies a large portion of the First Floor, characterized by narrow passages and open caverns lit only by phosphorescent fungi. The terrain favors ambushes, with hidden alcoves and debris providing cover for goblins.

## Inhabitants

Goblins dominate the district, from common warriors to shamans. They are highly territorial and coordinate pack attacks using the environment. Their lairs are scattered throughout the district, often protected by crude traps.
