# Crystal Cave

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Cave |
| **Location** | Labyrinth, First Floor |

The Crystal Cave is the first floor of the Labyrinth, a sprawling dungeon known for its crystalline formations. The cave is illuminated by natural crystals that line the walls and ceilings, casting a faint glow throughout the cavern. These crystals give the floor its name and create a unique environment that distinguishes it from deeper levels.

Beyond serving as the introductory level of the Labyrinth, the Crystal Cave holds specific importance as a training site. A particular cavern within the first floor is used by Tersia to train Erwen, honing her abilities amidst the crystal-rich surroundings. This location provides an ideal setting for practice, with its open spaces and natural obstacles.

The Crystal Cave is also the starting point for expeditions into the Labyrinth. Parties entering the dungeon must first traverse this crystalline floor before descending further. Its relative safety compared to deeper levels makes it a common area for preparation and initial exploration.

## Notable Events

In Chapter 28, Tersia utilizes the Crystal Cave as a training ground for Erwen, taking advantage of the crystal-rich environment to enhance her skills. Later, in Chapter 49, the party enters the Crystal Cave at the beginning of their expedition into the Labyrinth, marking the start of their descent.
