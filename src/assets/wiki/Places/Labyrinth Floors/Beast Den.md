The Beast Den is the second floor of the Labyrinth, a sprawling underground complex that adventurers must traverse. As its name suggests, this floor is primarily inhabited by a variety of hostile beasts, making it a dangerous and challenging environment for those who enter. The floor can be accessed only after passing through the portal on the first floor, and it serves as a natural progression for explorers seeking to delve deeper into the Labyrinth's depths. The Beast Den is characterized by its rugged terrain, which includes caves, tunnels, and open chambers that provide ample cover for its predatory inhabitants. The air is often thick with the sounds of roaring creatures and the scent of blood, a constant reminder of the perils that lurk within.

## Background

The Beast Den is known as the second floor of the Labyrinth, a multi-layered dungeon of unknown origin. It is one of the earliest floors encountered by adventurers, following directly after the first floor's entry portal. The floor is designed as a proving ground, testing the combat skills and survival instincts of those who venture into it.

## Inhabitants

The Beast Den is home to numerous species of aggressive beasts, ranging from small, swift predators to large, powerful monsters. These creatures have adapted to the floor's environment, often using the terrain to ambush intruders. The exact variety of beasts is not fully cataloged, but they are universally hostile and pose a constant threat to any party traversing the floor.
