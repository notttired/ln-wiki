# Commercial District

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Shopping District |
| **Location** | Rafdonia |

The Commercial District of Rafdonia is a bustling marketplace where adventurers and locals alike gather to acquire goods of all kinds. Known for its variety of wares, from weapons and armor to exotic accessories, the district attracts those seeking both everyday items and specialized equipment. The streets are lined with shops and stalls, each offering unique merchandise, and haggling is a common practice among buyers and sellers.

Bjorn, a notable figure in Rafdonia, visited the Commercial District to invest his earnings in new gear. He successfully bargained for a sturdy mace, paying 220,000 units, and purchased a half armor that fit him perfectly, costing 360,000 units. Additionally, he commissioned a custom helmet for 170,000 units, demonstrating the district's capacity for tailored orders. These transactions highlight the district's role as a key location for equipping adventurers.
