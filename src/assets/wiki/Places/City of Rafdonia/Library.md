The Library of Rafdonia stands as the city's foremost repository of written knowledge, housing vast collections spanning history, alchemy, monsters, and countless other subjects. Its towering shelves and quiet reading rooms attract scholars, mages, and students from across the region.

The library is overseen by the librarian Parsitiev, a skilled mage who employs a unique locator spell to assist patrons. When a reader thinks of a topic, the spell draws their attention to the most relevant books on the shelves, streamlining research significantly. This magical system is a hallmark of the institution.

Among its frequent visitors is the ascetic Bjorn, who has established a rigorous daily routine of studying within its walls from eight in the morning until four in the afternoon. His dedication has made him a familiar presence, and he has grown accustomed to the library's magical guidance over time.

## Background

The Library is a central institution in Rafdonia, serving as a hub for scholars and knowledge-seekers. Its extensive collection covers a wide range of disciplines, making it a valuable resource for all forms of research.

## Magical Systems

Parsitiev's magical book location system is a defining feature of the Library, allowing readers to find relevant texts with ease.

### Book Locator Spell

A spell that draws the user's attention to books related to their current thoughts, enabling efficient navigation of the Library's vast holdings.

## Daily Operations

The Library opens daily and sees regular use by dedicated readers. Notably, the ascetic Bjorn has established a strict schedule of studying from 8 AM to 4 PM, and has grown accustomed to the magical guidance provided by Parsitiev.
