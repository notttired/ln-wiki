The Underground Prison serves as the third chapter of the Bloodstained Fortress, a subterranean maze spanning three floors that evoke the atmosphere of a mad scientist's laboratory. The first floor is a dim corridor lined with iron bars, behind which hang chained dead men and skeletal rats scurry. The second floor transforms into a laboratory setting, housing twisted experiments such as chimera wolves and ghoul lords. The lowest floor is a circular, sewage-like chamber with large pipes dripping blackish filth; the ground is piled with bent limbs and severed heads. Monsters inhabiting the prison include Skull Rats, Banshees, Death Fiends, Chimera Wolves, Ghoul Lords, and Corpse Golems. Notably, a Corpse Golem is encountered by a party descending into this area.

## Appearance

The Underground Prison comprises three distinct floors, each with its own grim character.

### First Floor

A corridor lined with iron bars, behind which hang chained dead men; skeletal rats scurry about.

### Second Floor

Resembles a laboratory, hosting chimera wolves and ghoul lords.

### Third Floor

A circular, sewage-like chamber with large pipes dripping blackish filth; the floor is piled with bent limbs and severed heads.

## Inhabitants

The Underground Prison is home to a variety of monsters, including Skull Rats, Banshees, Death Fiends, Chimera Wolves, Ghoul Lords, and Corpse Golems.

## Notable Events

A party descending into the prison encounters a Corpse Golem, a powerful undead construct.
