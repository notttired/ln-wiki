# Demon Worship Hall

| | |
|---|---|

The Demon Worship Hall is the final chamber of the Bloodstained Fortress Rift, located deep beneath the lord's castle. This spacious stone room is adorned with intimidating demon sculptures and its floor is entirely covered in human skulls, creating an atmosphere of dread and dark reverence. The hall serves as the culmination of the Rift's trials, confronting those who enter with the stark evidence of the fortress's brutal history.

## Appearance

The Demon Worship Hall is a vast stone chamber featuring grotesque demon sculptures that line the walls, giving the space a menacing and unholy aura. The floor is paved with countless human skulls, a grim mosaic that underscores the hall's purpose as a site of dark worship or sacrifice.

## Location

The Demon Worship Hall is situated beneath the lord's castle, forming the deepest and most sacred part of the Bloodstained Fortress Rift. It marks the end point of the Rift's progression, accessible only after navigating the preceding chambers.
