Rifts are portals that appear at random intervals on the floors of the Labyrinth, each leading to a separate dungeon space known as an instance dungeon. These portals offer explorers a chance to challenge a contained environment with a powerful Guardian at its heart. Defeating the Guardian yields rare and valuable items that cannot be obtained elsewhere in the Labyrinth.

The opening of a Rift is governed by a cooldown period that lasts between three and eight cycles (months) after the previous Rift on the same floor. While they appear spontaneously, explorers can also force a Rift open by offering specific magic stones at the Monument to the Great Sage, a method employed by Bjorn and Ainar on the First Floor to seek greater strength. The forcible opening allows a party to enter a Rift on demand, bypassing the natural randomness.

The Guardian within a Rift drops a set of highly sought-after items: Numbers Items, which are believed to grant numerical enhancements; Guardian's Essence, a substance used in advanced crafting or empowerment; and Rift Stones, which may serve as keys or materials. The distribution of these spoils often leads to negotiation among expedition members, as seen during the Bloodstained Fortress expedition.

## Mechanics

Rifts open randomly on each floor, with a cooldown ranging from three to eight cycles after the previous appearance. They can also be forcibly opened by offering specific magic stones at the Monument to the Great Sage, a method used by explorers to enter on demand.

## Loot

The Guardian within a Rift drops a set of valuable items, each with unique properties. These are highly sought after by explorers.

### Numbers Item

Items that are believed to grant numerical enhancements to the wielder, making them coveted for their potential to increase combat effectiveness.

### Guardian's Essence

A potent substance used in advanced crafting or empowerment, essential for certain enhancements or creations within the Labyrinth.

### Rift Stone

A mysterious stone dropped by Guardians, possibly used as a key or material for further progression or crafting.
