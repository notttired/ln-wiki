# Cardinal Androne

| | |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Cardinal |
| **Era** | ~150 years ago |
| **Affiliation** | Church |

Cardinal Androne is a historical figure remembered for his pivotal role during the Dimensional Collapse approximately 150 years ago. As a high-ranking member of the Church, he held the title of Cardinal and was entrusted with the three sacred artifacts known as the Tears of the Goddess.

During the cataclysmic Dimensional Collapse, Androne invoked the power of all three Tears to save the Pope, an act of immense sacrifice and faith. His successful use of the artifacts came at a cost: immediately after the rescue, the Tears of the Goddess vanished without a trace, their whereabouts unknown for over a century.

Androne's legacy endures through scattered historical records. Raven's book, mentioned in passing by scholars, notes Androne as the last known wielder of the Tears. His story symbolizes both the might of divine artifacts and the mysteries that shroud the Collapse era.

## Background

Cardinal Androne served the Church during the tumultuous period of the Dimensional Collapse, a time when the fabric of reality was threatened. His exact origins are unrecorded, but his rank indicates a lifetime of devotion and ascent within the ecclesiastical hierarchy.

## Historical Significance

Androne is uniquely remembered as the only individual known to have wielded all three Tears of the Goddess simultaneously. His actions during the Collapse prevented a disaster of unknown scale and saved the life of the Pope. The subsequent disappearance of the Tears has made his feat a subject of fascination and speculation among historians and treasure hunters alike.
