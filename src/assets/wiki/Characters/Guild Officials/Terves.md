# Terves

| | |
|---|---|
| **Real Name** | Terves |
| **Occupation** | Guild Administrator |
| **Affiliation** | Explorer's Guild |
| **Location** | Explorer's Guild near the Mage Tower |

Terves is a guild administrator at the Explorer's Guild located near the Mage Tower. Known for his impeccable politeness and efficiency, he handles the registration and processing of adventurer requests. When the sorcerer Bjorn sought companions for an expedition, it was Terves who processed his paperwork. His mannerisms, however, struck Bjorn as overly perfect and slightly unsettling, leaving an impression of someone who operates with mechanical precision rather than genuine warmth. Despite this, Terves performs his duties without error, ensuring that all guild procedures are followed to the letter.

## Personality

Terves exhibits an overly polite and efficient demeanor that borders on mechanical. His perfect mannerisms and lack of any visible flaw in his conduct make him appear almost inhuman, a quality that unsettles those who interact with him, such as the sorcerer Bjorn.

## Notable Encounters

Terves' most documented interaction is with the sorcerer Bjorn, who visited the Explorer's Guild to register for finding party members.

### Bjorn

A sorcerer who sought companions at the Explorer's Guild. Terves processed his request with flawless politeness, but his mechanical demeanor left Bjorn feeling uneasy.
