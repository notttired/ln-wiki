# Niall Urbans

| | |
|---|---|

| **Status** | Active |

Niall Urbans serves as the Regional Chief of the Explorer's Guild, holding the position of second-in-command below the Guild Master. As a high-ranking official, he oversees regional operations and is responsible for maintaining order and handling crises within the Guild's jurisdiction. His political acumen is demonstrated during his handling of the prison break and hostage crisis at the Guild's detention facility.

In the aftermath of the incident, Urbans is forced into negotiations with the escaped prisoner and Rank 6 explorer Bjorn Yandel. Faced with a hostage situation and a compromising recording, Urbans attempts to bribe Arua Raven, a fellow explorer and associate of Yandel, into selling a Crystal Orb to secure leverage. When the recording is used against him, he concedes to all of Yandel's demands: the dismissal of the investigator assigned to the case, the sentencing of fellow explorer Hartz Young to death, the promotion of Bjorn Yandel to Rank 7, and payment of 5 million Stones as compensation. These actions reveal a pragmatic approach to conflict, prioritizing de-escalation and damage control over confrontation.

Urbans's willingness to negotiate under duress and his attempt at bribery highlight a politically flexible nature. While his decisions resolve the immediate crisis, they also raise questions about the Guild's internal stability and the extent of his authority. As Regional Chief, his actions set a precedent for how the Guild responds to threats from within its own ranks.

## Background

Niall Urbans is the Regional Chief of the Explorer's Guild, second only to the Guild Master. He oversees regional operations and is responsible for maintaining order and handling major incidents within the Guild's jurisdiction. His position requires both administrative skill and political savvy, as he must balance the interests of the Guild with the actions of its members.

## Personality

Urbans exhibits a strong sense of political pragmatism. During the prison break hostage crisis, he first attempts to bribe Arua Raven to recover a compromising item, then swiftly capitulates to all demands when the recording is used against him. This indicates a willingness to use underhanded tactics and a priority on resolving conflicts through negotiation rather than force, even at significant cost.
