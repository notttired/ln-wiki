# Baron Martoan

Baron Martoan is a noble house that holds a barony within the realm. Little is known about the house itself, as it is only referenced indirectly. The house is notable primarily due to Dwalki's claim of distant kinship: he asserts that his aunt's husband's brother is a member of the Martoan family. This connection is used by Dwalki to boast about his status, though the veracity and closeness of the relation remain unverified. The Martoan barony thus appears in records only through this tenuous link, and its actual domains and influence are not documented.

## Background

Baron Martoan first appears in records during Chapter 49, where it is mentioned solely through Dwalki's boast of a familial connection. No further details about its territory, history, or other members are known.
