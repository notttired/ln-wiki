Julian Urbans is the daughter of the Regional Chief. She was taken hostage by Bjorn during his escape from the Explorer's Guild prison. Despite the danger, she remained calm and attempted to negotiate with Bjorn, demonstrating a strong will and quick thinking. Her composure under pressure allowed her to assess the situation and engage in dialogue, seeking a peaceful resolution. Her status as the daughter of the Regional Chief made her a valuable bargaining chip in Bjorn's attempt to secure the Guild's cooperation.
