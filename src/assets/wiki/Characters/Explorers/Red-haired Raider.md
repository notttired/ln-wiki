The Red-haired Raider is a dangerous explorer hunter operating on the Eighth Floor of the Labyrinth. Known for preying on other explorers rather than monsters, she has earned a reputation as a ruthless and efficient killer. Standing over 170 centimeters with a lean build, her most distinctive features are her shoulder-length red hair, a tattoo beneath her eye, and the missing top half of her right ear. She never wears a mask, a testament to her confidence that her victims will not survive to report her.

Her primary method of killing involves the use of Basilisk Paralysis Poison applied to a dagger. This poison causes rapid muscle stiffening and loss of strength, allowing her to incapacitate victims without leaving external injuries. She carries a magical tool bag capable of storing bulky items, which she uses to loot gear from her victims. Despite her profession, she has shown a code of conduct: when she encountered the explorers Bjorn and Ainar after already killing four others, she chose to ignore them initially. However, after they witnessed her looting, she pursued them relentlessly.

The Red-haired Raider forced Bjorn and Ainar to swear an Oath of silence, revealing that she is from the Eighth Floor and owes a debt to the Barbarians. For reasons tied to this debt, she spared their lives and even used a high-grade potion to heal their wounds and reverse the paralysis. Her connection to the Barbarians remains unclear, but it suggests she operates with some degree of allegiance or obligation beyond simple banditry.

## Appearance

The Red-haired Raider is a lean woman standing over 170 cm tall, with shoulder-length red hair. She has a tattoo under one eye and the top half of her right ear is missing. She does not wear a mask, indicating her confidence in not being identified or reported.

## Equipment

The Red-haired Raider carries several notable items used for hunting explorers and looting their gear.

### Dagger

A bloodied dagger used to apply Basilisk Paralysis Poison. It is her primary weapon for incapacitating victims.

### Basilisk Paralysis Poison

A poison that causes rapid muscle stiffening and loss of strength, paralyzing victims. It allows her to kill without leaving external injuries.

### Magical Tool Bag

A magical bag capable of storing bulky items, used to carry loot from victims.

### High-grade Potion

A powerful healing potion used on Bjorn and Ainar to heal their wounds and reverse the paralysis, demonstrating her access to high-quality resources.

## Combat Methods

The Red-haired Raider relies on stealth, poison, and surprise. She prefers to ambush explorers, using her basilisk poison to paralyze them before they can fight back. She demonstrates patience and confidence, prioritizing the looting of corpses even when witnesses are present, as seen when she continued looting while being observed by Bjorn and Ainar.

## Relationships

The Red-haired Raider has two significant relationships that influence her actions.

### Barbarians

She claims to owe a debt to the Barbarians, which motivated her to spare Bjorn and Ainar by offering an Oath instead of killing them. The nature of this debt is unknown.

### Bjorn and Ainar

She encountered these two explorers on the Eighth Floor. After they witnessed her looting dead explorers, she pursued them, forced them to swear an Oath of silence, and then healed their wounds and paralysis with a high-grade potion.
