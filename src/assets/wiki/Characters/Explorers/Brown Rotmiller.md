Brown Rotmiller, commonly known as Rotmiller, is a Human Seventh Rank Scout with eight years of exploration experience in the Labyrinth. As the most veteran member of his team, he takes charge of navigation and leads the party through the First Floor. His long survival in the dangerous depths despite his low rank speaks to his reliance on caution and practical expertise rather than raw power. Rotmiller is noted for his serious and inflexible demeanor, traits forged by years of harsh conditions that demand discipline and strict adherence to survival protocols. Among his companions, he is regarded as the only normal person, a grounded presence in an otherwise unusual group.

## Background

Rotmiller has spent eight years exploring the Labyrinth, accumulating extensive knowledge of its dangers and navigation. Despite only reaching the Seventh Rank, his prolonged survival is a testament to his experience and cautious approach. He joins the team as a veteran scout, assuming responsibility for guiding them through the First Floor.

## Personality

Rotmiller is serious and inflexible, qualities likely honed by the unforgiving environment of the Labyrinth. He adheres to strict protocols and expects the same from his teammates. His no-nonsense attitude makes him the only normal person on the team, providing a stabilizing counterbalance to the group's more volatile members.
