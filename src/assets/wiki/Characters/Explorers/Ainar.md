Ainar is a female Barbarian explorer and the second daughter of the renowned Fenelin. Hailing from the northern barbarian tribes, she is a powerful warrior who wields a greatsword with considerable skill. Ainar's journey takes a decisive turn when she partners with Bjorn, pledging her loyalty through the Warrior's Honor and becoming his steadfast companion. Her physical prowess is enhanced after absorbing the Bone Knight Essence, which grants her increased cutting power, bone density, agility, and endurance at the cost of flexibility and height. Ainar has proven herself in battle, notably against the Death Fiend and the Vampire Duke, where she drew aggro and supported the party. After the Vampire Duke's defeat, she cared for an unconscious Bjorn for three days, providing comfort and refusing to leave his side. Seeking to grow further, she later decides to formally learn swordsmanship under an elder at the Holy Land for six months, leaving Bjorn temporarily.

## Background

Ainar is the second daughter of Fenelin, a prominent figure among the barbarians. She initially struggled with illiteracy and recognized the need for knowledge, leading her to plan a visit to the Holy Land to learn reading. In the Library, she encountered Bjorn and later accepted his proposal to become his companion with an 8:2 split in his favor. She followed him into the Labyrinth using the Binding Spell and has remained loyal ever since.

## Personality

Ainar is a proud warrior who takes her warrior's honor seriously. She is determined and open to growth, as shown by her decision to learn reading despite the difficulty. She exhibits a strong sense of loyalty, pledging to Bjorn without question and altruistically helping other barbarians navigate the Labyrinth. However, she struggles with pride and humiliation, as seen when she was forced to swear an Oath to save her life, feeling deeply ashamed. Despite her brave exterior, she shows fear of supernatural undead like Banshees, clenching her jaw and clinging to Bjorn when frightened, yet still manages to fight courageously. Her composure under pressure is demonstrated when she calmly grabbed a Dwarf and lifted him away from an explosive corpse attack.

## Abilities and Equipment

Ainar is a highly skilled swordsman, capable of defeating most opponents in sparring within minutes. She uses a greatsword that can cleave through skeletons and deal significant damage to eighth-rank monsters. Her combat style, while predictable, is intense and effective. She is open to advice but struggles to change ingrained habits.

### Essence Abilities

By absorbing the Bone Knight Essence, Ainar gained enhanced physical abilities and unique powers.

#### Bone Knight Essence

A Yearning-type essence absorbed by Ainar, granting increased cutting power, bone density, agility, and endurance, while reducing flexibility and height. It also provides active Life Drain and passive Preservative abilities.

## Relationships

Ainar's most significant relationship is with Bjorn, her companion and leader. She pledged loyalty by Warrior's Honor and follows him without question. She trusts him with resource distribution (8:2 split) and respects his wisdom, even insisting he should lead the party over the Mage. After Bjorn fell unconscious following the Vampire Duke battle, she cared for him for three days, providing a lap pillow, clothing him with a makeshift skirt, and waiting until he awoke. Her decision to leave for training at the Holy Land is a temporary separation.
