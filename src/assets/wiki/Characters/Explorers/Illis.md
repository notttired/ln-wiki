# Illis

| | |
|---|---|
| **Real Name** | Illis |
| **Vital Status** | Deceased |
| **Occupation** | Samurai |
| **Affiliation** | Group of Four Explorers |

Illis is a samurai and one of a party of four explorers. He is a skilled swordsman, though little else is known of his background or his comrades. In combat with Bjorn, his abilities prove insufficient against the overwhelming strength of his opponent. Illis is ultimately slain when Bjorn sinks his teeth into his neck, a fatal wound that ends the samurai's life.
