Misha Karlstein is a Beastkin explorer and warrior of Bjorn's team. She is the princess of the Red Beast Tribe, one of the five great Beastkin tribes, and the daughter of the tribe's clan chief. Her appearance is striking: tall and slender with triangular ears and an aloof aura that creates a cold atmosphere around her. She speaks with a distinct slur at the ends of her words, which she claims is a natural consequence of Beastkin anatomy. Despite her royal lineage, Misha serves as a frontline combatant, joining the team in the dangerous work of exploration. Introduced in Chapter 49, she quickly establishes herself as a capable warrior, her royal bearing tempered by the grit required of an explorer.

## Background

Misha is the princess of the Red Beast Tribe, one of the five great tribes of the Beastkin. Her father serves as the tribe's clan chief. She was introduced as a Beastkin explorer and joined Bjorn's team as a warrior in Chapter 49.

## Personality

Misha carries an aloof demeanor that often gives others a cold impression. She speaks with a habit of slurring only the ends of her words, which she attributes to her Beastkin anatomy. This speech pattern is a distinctive trait that sets her apart.

## Appearance

Misha stands tall, in the early 170s centimeters, with a long and slender build. She has triangular Beastkin ears. Her overall appearance contributes to an aloof atmosphere that matches her personality.
