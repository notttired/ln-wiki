# Mr. Patran

| | |
|---|---|
| **Real Name** | Mr. Patran |
| **Occupation** | Explorer |

Mr. Patran is an explorer known for being reachable through Crystal Orb communication. He provided Arua Raven with critical information regarding the consequences of forcing an unplanned Essence onto an individual, stating that such an act requires compensation of 15 million Stone or results in death. This consultation occurred during Raven's investigation into Essence-related matters, supporting arguments against forced Essence assignment.
