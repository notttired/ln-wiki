Persi was a crossbowman who served as part of a group of four explorers active in the region. Little is known of his background or motivations prior to his final confrontation. He participated in an ambush against Bjorn and Erwen, indicating he was allied with hostile forces operating in the area. During the skirmish, Erwen loosed an arrow that struck Persi, killing him instantly. His death marked the end of his involvement in the conflict, and no further records of his exploits exist beyond this encounter.

## Death

Persi was killed in chapter 15 by Erwen's arrow during an ambush. He was part of a group of four explorers that attacked Bjorn and Erwen. The exact circumstances of the encounter suggest a premeditated assault, but Persi did not survive to provide his account.
