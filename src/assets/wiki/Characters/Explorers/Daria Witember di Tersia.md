Daria Witember di Tersia is a fifth-year Fairy Explorer with distinctive silver hair and a reputation for unwavering protectiveness. As the elder sister of Erwen, she takes an active role in her sibling's development, personally training her on the First Floor in preparation for the imminent Rift opening. Her commitment to Erwen's progress is evident in her decisive actions during their first joint appearance, where she retrieves Erwen from the Paidenfuss tavern by paying Bjorn his share and integrating her sister into her party.

Daria's cautious nature defines her approach to both adventuring and mentorship. When she overhears rumors concerning the Barbarian of Freedom, she consciously chooses not to relay the information to Erwen, judging that the distraction would hinder her sister's focus. This decision underscores her priority on Erwen's success and safety, even at the cost of withholding potentially important news. Her actions reveal a pragmatist who values preparation and mental clarity over curiosity.

Despite her vigilance, Daria maintains a composed and resolute demeanor. She navigates her role as a senior student and guardian with a sense of duty, ensuring that Erwen is both mentally and physically ready for the challenges ahead. While her appearances are limited, each instance reinforces her dedication to her sister and her commitment to the responsibilities of a Fairy Explorer.

## Background

Daria is a fifth-year student at the Fairy Explorer academy, which places her among the more experienced trainees. She holds the rank of Fairy Explorer, a guild designation that signifies her trained status and commitment to exploring the First Floor and beyond. Her upbringing alongside her younger sister Erwen has fostered a strong bond, and she has taken it upon herself to oversee Erwen's growth as an explorer.

## Personality

Daria is characterized by her protective and cautious nature. She prioritizes her sister's well-being and training above all else, as demonstrated when she withholds news of the Barbarian of Freedom to prevent distraction. Her decisions are deliberate and aimed at long-term goals, showing a maturity befitting her seniority. While reserved, she is not hesitant to act when necessary, as seen in her efficient handling of the tavern situation.

## Relationships

Daria's most significant relationship is with her younger sister, Erwen. She acts as both mentor and guardian, guiding Erwen through the dangers of the First Floor. Daria is deeply invested in Erwen's success and often makes choices that prioritize her sister's focus and safety. Their bond is one of mutual reliance, with Daria shouldering the responsibility of preparation while Erwen hones her skills under Daria's watchful eye.

## Notable Actions

In Chapter 17, Daria arrives at the Paidenfuss tavern to retrieve her sister, paying Bjorn for his share of the party and formally taking Erwen into her own group. Chapter 28 depicts her actively training Erwen on the First Floor, drilling her for the approaching Rift. In Chapter 45, she overhears a conversation about the Barbarian of Freedom and decides against informing her sister, valuing Erwen's uninterrupted training over potential knowledge.
