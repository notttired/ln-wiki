Deian Tveherion is a senior mage and the master of the Altemion School, a prestigious institution of magical learning. Despite his advanced age, marked by white hair and deeply wrinkled skin, he carries himself with a spryness that belies his years. He is first encountered in Raven's laboratory, where he arrives to oversee matters and offers the Frost Spirit Ring as compensation to Bjorn. His demeanor is jovial and warm, often punctuated by hearty laughter and lighthearted banter, yet beneath this affable exterior lies a sharp, calculating mind. He intentionally matches Bjorn's high tension during their interaction, later revealing that he sought to confirm a particular suspicion about the young mage.

## Appearance

Deian Tveherion appears as an elderly man with pure white hair and heavily wrinkled skin, yet his posture is straight and his movements are remarkably spry for his age. There is no sign of frailty in his build, and he carries himself with the vitality of a much younger individual.

## Personality

Deian possesses a jovial personality, frequently laughing heartily and making lighthearted comments, such as joking about dead barbarians during his meeting with Bjorn. He engages in friendly banter and maintains a warm, approachable demeanor. However, he is also profoundly calculating; his actions are deliberate, and he reveals that he matched Bjorn's tension with a specific purpose in mind—to confirm something about Bjorn's nature or abilities.
