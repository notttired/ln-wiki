The Immortal Engraving is a high-rank Spirit Engraving on the Immortal Path, renowned for its ability to greatly enhance the bearer's physical form. It costs 800,000 Stone, though Bjorn received a 200,000 Stone gift from the Tribe Chief to help cover the expense. The engraving imparts a significant increase in natural regeneration and bestows a +20 bonus to all physical stats. It serves as the cornerstone of Bjorn's training method, allowing him to push his body beyond its normal limits. The procedure is performed by the Shaman, who uses needles to apply the engraving following a series of tattoos. Stage 1 of the Immortal Engraving has been activated, marking the beginning of Bjorn's transformation.

## Effects

The Immortal Engraving greatly enhances natural regeneration and provides a +20 bonus to all physical stats. These improvements allow the bearer to recover from injuries and exertions more quickly and to achieve greater physical prowess.

## Procedure

The engraving is applied by the Shaman using special needles that follow pre-drawn tattoos. The process is described as intensely painful, but results in a surge of energy upon completion. Only Stage 1 has been activated so far.

## Cost and Acquisition

The engraving costs 800,000 Stone, a significant sum. Bjorn received a 200,000 Stone gift from the Tribe Chief to help defray some of the cost.
