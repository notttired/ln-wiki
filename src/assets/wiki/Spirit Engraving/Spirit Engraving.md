Spirit Engraving is a unique growth method practiced exclusively by the Barbarian race. It is an enhancement art that infuses the body with Soul Power through the application of magical ingredients into tattoos. This art is considered a defining characteristic of the Barbarians, similar to how Fairies handle spirits and Dwarves handle Artifacts. All Barbarians receive Spirit Engraving as newborns, with their tattoos designed to keep the soul circuits visible and active throughout their lives.

The process is performed by the Tribe Chief and the Shaman, who use needles to apply the Soul Power-laden ingredients into the existing tattoo patterns. The procedure is notoriously painful, but it grants the recipient special powers and abilities. There are multiple paths within Spirit Engraving, each offering different enhancements; Bjorn, for instance, chose the Immortal Path. The cost of Spirit Engraving is substantial, with Bjorn planning to spend his 1.4 million Stones on the procedure.

The tattoos themselves are not merely decorative but serve as conduits for Soul Power, allowing the Barbarian to grow stronger and unlock unique abilities. The art is central to Barbarian culture and their warrior tradition, marking every member of the race from birth.

## Background

Spirit Engraving is a hallmark of Barbarian identity, comparable to the Fairies' affinity for spirits and the Dwarves' mastery of artifacts. It is a costly and sacred practice that every Barbarian undergoes as a newborn to ensure their soul circuits remain visible and functional throughout life.

## Mechanics

The infusion process involves the Tribe Chief and Shaman using needles to apply magical ingredients rich in Soul Power into the pre-existing tattoo patterns of the recipient. This causes intense pain but effectively embeds Soul Power into the body, enhancing the Barbarian's capabilities. The tattoos serve as conduits to maintain and channel the Soul Power.

## Paths

Spirit Engraving offers multiple distinct paths, each granting unique enhancements and abilities. Bjorn, a notable Barbarian, selected the Immortal Path, which likely confers longevity or regeneration-related powers. Further paths exist but are not detailed.

## Cost

The procedure is financially demanding; Bjorn allocated 1.4 million Stones from his funds to undergo Spirit Engraving. This high cost reflects the rarity and potency of the magical ingredients required.
