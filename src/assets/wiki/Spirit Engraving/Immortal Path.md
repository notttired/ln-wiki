# Immortal Path

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Spirit Engraving Path |
| **Chosen By** | Bjorn |
| **First Appearance** | Chapter 21.0 |

The Immortal Path is one of the many paths available within the discipline of Spirit Engraving, a practice that allows practitioners to inscribe spiritual power onto themselves. It was specifically chosen by Bjorn for his own Spirit Engraving due to its reputation for high performance and its strong connection to his unique Training Method. The path is said to grant superior abilities, making it a coveted choice among those who seek peak efficiency in spiritual cultivation. While the exact techniques and effects of the Immortal Path are not widely documented, its selection by Bjorn highlights its significance in enhancing combat prowess and spiritual refinement. The path and Bjorn's Training Method are deeply intertwined, with each complementing the other to produce remarkable results. As a result, the Immortal Path stands as a key component of Bjorn's approach to harnessing spiritual energy, though its full capabilities remain known only to him and those who study his methods closely.

## Overview

The Immortal Path is a Spirit Engraving path characterized by high performance and synergy with specialized training methods. It was chosen by Bjorn for his Spirit Engraving, as noted in Chapter 21.0.
