The Lithium Alloy Breastplate is a Tier 2 piece of armor forged from a white lithium alloy, prized for its exceptional lightness and hardness. The armor was acquired by Bjorn for 1,910,000 Stone, a substantial investment aimed at providing maximal protection to his heart. Its design synergizes with Bjorn's Source of Darkness passive ability, allowing the breastplate to function as a critical component of his defensive strategy. The breastplate's unique material composition ensures that it does not hinder movement while offering robust resistance against physical attacks, making it a valuable asset in combat.

## Background

Bjorn purchased the Lithium Alloy Breastplate in Chapter 47 specifically to enhance his defensive capabilities. The armor's primary function is to safeguard his heart, aligning with the requirements of his Source of Darkness passive. The transaction cost 1,910,000 Stone, reflecting the breastplate's high quality and specialized nature.

## Properties

The breastplate is constructed from a lithium alloy, a material known for its low density and high strength. It is white in color and classified as Tier 2 equipment. Its lightness does not compromise its hardness, allowing it to absorb significant impacts without weighing down the wearer.

## Synergy

The breastplate's effect is amplified when used in conjunction with Bjorn's Source of Darkness passive ability. This synergy maximizes the protection of his heart, ensuring that the armor's defensive potential is fully realized in battle.
