The Lithium Alloy battle Shield is a large, Tier 2 defensive item crafted from lightweight yet durable lithium alloy. Purchased by Bjorn for 2,100,000 Stone, the shield is designed to enable its wielder to become a human wall, providing robust protection for companions in battle. Its high cost reflects its quality and the enhanced defensive capabilities it offers. The shield's lithium alloy construction makes it both strong and relatively light, allowing for sustained use in prolonged engagements. Bjorn acquired it with the explicit intention of bolstering his role as a frontline protector.
