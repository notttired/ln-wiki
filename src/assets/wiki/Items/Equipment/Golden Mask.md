# Golden Mask

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Consumable Helmet |
| **Value** | 500,000 Stone |
| **Properties** | |
| Uses | 5 (max) |
| Duration | 30 days |

The Golden Mask is a unique consumable helmet renowned for its ability to alter the wearer's facial appearance. When worn, it grants the user complete control over their facial features, allowing for a customized look that lasts for thirty days. However, the mask's power is finite; it can be used a maximum of five times before it vanishes. This ephemeral nature makes it a prized yet temporary tool for those seeking to change their identity or appearance.

The mask is obtained by defeating the boss of the Bloodstained Fortress and fulfilling additional special conditions. The exact method of acquisition is not widely known, adding to the mask's mystique. Once obtained, it is a highly valuable item on the Exchange, where it trades for approximately 500,000 Stone.

In the ancient tome that lists rare artifacts, the Golden Mask appears alongside the Tear of the Goddess, though the details of its entry were illegible. This mention suggests that the mask has long been recognized as a significant magical item. Its limited uses and high value make it a treasure sought by collectors and adventurers alike.

## Effect

The Golden Mask allows the wearer to customize their facial appearance for up to thirty consecutive days. It is a consumable item that vanishes after its maximum of five uses is exhausted.
