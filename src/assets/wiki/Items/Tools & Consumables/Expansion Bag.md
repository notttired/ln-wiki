The Expansion Bag is an Eighth Rank storage backpack renowned for its capacity and convenience, purchased for 2,500,000 Stone. It features three magical enchantments that streamline inventory management: Auto-Sort, Individual Withdrawal, and Lighten (Low). These enchantments automatically organize stored items, allow the user to retrieve specific items without sifting through the entire bag, and reduce the effective weight of its contents, respectively. The bag's rank and price reflect its high quality and utility, making it a valuable asset for any adventurer seeking to carry vast quantities of equipment and loot without encumbrance.

Acquired in Chapter 47, the Expansion Bag replaced lesser storage solutions, offering a significant upgrade in both capacity and ease of access. Its owner, an experienced hunter, recognized the need for a more efficient way to carry resources during expeditions. The bag's enchantments work in harmony to ensure that items are always neatly arranged and readily available, reducing time spent managing inventory in the field.

## Acquisition

The Expansion Bag was purchased for 2,500,000 Stone in Chapter 47, providing a substantial increase in inventory capacity and convenience.
