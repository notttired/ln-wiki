Corpse Flowers are a hardy species of flora native to the Land of the Dead, where they grow on vines that cover the rubble of collapsed stone buildings. The flowers themselves are distinctive, though their appearance is seldom remarked upon due to the desolation of the region. The true value of Corpse Flowers lies in their ability to provide potable water when the blossoms are split open, a fact that makes them a vital resource for any traveler or inhabitant of this barren wasteland.

As the only known source of fresh water in the Land of the Dead, Corpse Flowers are central to survival in the area. However, their use is not without controversy. Explorer Bjorn explained that these flowers are the sole water source in the region, but his companion Ainar expressed skepticism about their hygiene and questioned the safety of drinking water obtained from them. Despite these concerns, the flowers remain a critical, albeit unconventional, lifeline.

The availability of Corpse Flowers is tied to a blooming cycle, meaning they are not always present. Those who depend on them must be aware of the timing of this cycle to ensure access to water. The knowledge of when and where to find blooming Corpse Flowers is a closely guarded secret among the locals, as it can mean the difference between life and death in the arid Land of the Dead.

## Appearance

Corpse Flowers grow on thick vines that creep over the rubble of stone buildings in the Land of the Dead. The flowers themselves bloom from these vines, their exact coloration and shape not widely detailed but recognized by their unique role as a water source.

## Usage

When a Corpse Flower is split open, it yields a quantity of drinkable water. This water is the only reliable source of hydration in the Land of the Dead, making the flower indispensable for survival. The extraction process is simple: the blossom is cracked open to release the liquid within. Some travelers express concern over the water's purity, but no instances of harm from its consumption have been recorded.

## Blooming Cycle

Corpse Flowers are not perpetually available; they bloom according to a specific cycle. Individuals seeking water must track this cycle to find flowers at the right stage. The exact length and triggers of the cycle are not widely known, adding to the challenge of surviving in the Land of the Dead.
