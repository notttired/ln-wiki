# Rift Stone

| | |
|---|---|
| **Origin** | Rift |
| **Function** | Loot allocation token |

A Rift Stone is a special item recovered from a Rift, distinguished by its use in loot allocation among adventurers. When a party defeats a Rift boss or completes a Rift objective, the Rift Stone allows the group to determine who receives priority on the resulting essences and other rewards. Its exact appearance and properties vary, but its function as a negotiation token is consistent across known instances.

In one notable incident, the warrior Bjorn initially claimed the Rift Stone for himself during a loot distribution. Recognizing the stone's value as a bargaining chip, he later relinquished his claim in exchange for first pick of the essences dropped from the Rift. This trade allowed the party to proceed with a more agreeable allocation, demonstrating the stone's role in facilitating party cohesion and strategic reward management.

The Rift Stone thus serves as a key tool for adventuring parties, enabling flexible and mutually beneficial distribution of spoils from Rift expeditions.

## Background

Rift Stones originate from the dimensional tears known as Rifts, which produce unique items and materials. They are specifically designed to aid in the equitable division of loot, acting as a token that can be assigned to a party member who then gains the right to negotiate or trade for other items. In practice, the stone functions as a placeholder for a share of the overall rewards, allowing parties to avoid conflicts over valuable essences and equipment.

## Notable History

During a Rift expedition, the warrior Bjorn initially demanded the Rift Stone as part of his share. However, he later agreed to forfeit the stone in exchange for priority rights on the essences produced by the Rift. This transaction highlights the stone's utility as a negotiation tool, as Bjorn valued the essences more than the stone itself, and the party was able to finalize their loot distribution without further dispute.
