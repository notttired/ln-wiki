# Message Stone

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Magical Communication Tool |
| **Range** | 300 meters |

Message Stones are magical tools used for short-range communication. When two stones are resonated together beforehand, they allow the users to communicate over a distance of up to 300 meters. The stones are typically used by groups that need to coordinate movements, such as the Crystal Alliance during their pursuit of targets. In chapter 11, Bjorn came into possession of one such stone after defeating Serdin, taking it from the corpse. This allowed him to potentially intercept or participate in the Alliance's communications. The stones have limited range and require prior attunement, making them useful only for pre-organized teams.

## Function

Message Stones function as paired communication devices. Each stone must be resonated with another specific stone to establish a link. Once linked, users can communicate with each other within a range of approximately 300 meters. The stones do not work with unpaired stones and have no other known functions.

## Notable History

The Crystal Alliance is known to have used Message Stones to coordinate their pursuit operations. In chapter 11, after the death of Serdin, Bjorn recovered a Message Stone from the corpse, thereby gaining access to the Alliance's communication network.
