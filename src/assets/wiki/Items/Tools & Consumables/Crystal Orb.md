# Crystal Orb

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Magical Communication Device |
| **Owner** | Arua Raven |
| **First Appearance** | Chapter 41 |

The Crystal Orb is a magical communication device that enables its user to call and speak with an explorer. It is in the possession of Arua Raven, who utilized it to contact Mr. Patran. During an incident involving the forcing of an Essence, Raven employed the orb to verify the consequences of such an act, demonstrating its utility as a tool for consultation with distant individuals. The orb appears to be a dedicated item for long-distance communication, likely enchanted to facilitate voice transmission.

## Notable Usage

The only recorded use of the Crystal Orb occurs in Chapter 41, when Arua Raven contacts the explorer Mr. Patran to inquire about the effects of forcibly assimilating an Essence. This shows the orb's role as a means of accessing expert knowledge from afar.
