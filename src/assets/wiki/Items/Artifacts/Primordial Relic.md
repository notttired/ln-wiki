# Primordial Relic

| | |
|---|---|
| **Required For** | Gate of the Abyss |
| **Total Number** | 6 |
| **Status** | Stolen |
| **Original Keeper** | Unknown (one of six tribes) |

Primordial Relics are six ancient artifacts of immense power, each entrusted to one of the world's six major tribes: Dwarves, Barbarians, Fairies, Beastkin, Dragonkin, and Humans. They are the only known keys capable of opening the Gate of the Abyss, a mysterious portal of unknown origin and purpose. The relics have been safeguarded by their respective tribes for generations, their locations and guardians closely guarded secrets. Recently, on the day of the Coming of Age Ceremony for Bjorn and Ainar, one of these relics was stolen. The theft was discovered immediately, but the perpetrator and the current whereabouts of the relic remain unknown. This incident has thrown the delicate balance among the tribes into uncertainty, as it is unclear whether other relics have also been compromised. For Bjorn, the loss of the relic represents a critical setback, as he had been seeking to use the Gate of the Abyss as a means of escape from the world.

## Background

The Primordial Relics are six artifacts that have been passed down through the generations, one per major tribe. Each tribe holds its relic in secret, with only a few trusted individuals knowing its location. The relics are said to predate recorded history and are the sole means to unlock the Gate of the Abyss, a portal whose destination or function is not widely known.

## Recent Events

On the day of Bjorn and Ainar's Coming of Age Ceremony, a Primordial Relic was stolen from its keeper tribe. The theft occurred amidst the celebrations, and the identity of the thief remains a mystery. This development has heightened tensions among the tribes, as the loss of even one relic could destabilize the balance of power and jeopardize the possibility of opening the Gate of the Abyss. The stolen relic's tribe has not been officially disclosed, raising concerns that more relics may be at risk.

## Significance

The Primordial Relics are the only known keys to the Gate of the Abyss. Without all six, the Gate cannot be opened. The recent theft has made the prospect of opening the Gate more uncertain than ever. For Bjorn, who viewed the Gate as his only potential escape from this world, the loss is a devastating blow that may force him to seek alternative paths.
