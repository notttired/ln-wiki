Essence of the Death Fiend is a rare essence obtained by slaying Death Fiends, a type of undead monster. This essence contains two inherent skills: the Active Skill 'Call of the Dead' and the Passive Skill 'Body Preservation'. While both skills are useful, the passive 'Body Preservation' is particularly sought after due to its synergy with the Immortal Engraving.

The Immortal Engraving provides a regeneration boost; when combined with Body Preservation, the effect is amplified, making the wielder significantly more resilient in battle. This combination is highly valued by those who rely on sustained combat or regenerative abilities.

Notably, Bjorn, a warrior known for his Immortal Engraving, attempted to obtain this essence to enhance his regeneration. However, after defeating seventy Death Fiends, the essence did not drop, underscoring its rarity.

## Skills

The Essence of the Death Fiend grants two skills, one active and one passive, each with distinct applications in combat and survival.

### Call of the Dead

An active skill that allows the user to summon undead minions or call upon the power of the dead in battle.
