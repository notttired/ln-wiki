The Vampire (Guardian) Essence is a powerful Guardian-type essence obtained from the Vampire Duke Cambormir. It is a crystallization of the duke's dark power, embodying the predatory and immortal nature of high-ranking vampires. As a Guardian essence, it provides its wielder with a suite of abilities that enhance survivability, combat effectiveness, and strategic versatility, particularly favoring those who employ a calculated, resource-based fighting style.

The essence grants four distinct skills: the passive Source of Darkness, and the active Sacrificial Mark, Immortal One, and Blood Master. Source of Darkness subtly influences the bearer's equipment choices, while the active skills offer tactical options ranging from marking targets to invoking near-immortality and manipulating blood. The synergy between these skills allows for aggressive yet sustained engagement, drawing upon the essence's ancient vampire lineage.

In practice, the Vampire (Guardian) Essence shapes its user's combat approach around careful resource management and controlled aggression. The passive Source of Darkness, for instance, has been observed to affect gear selection, encouraging items that complement dark or shadow-based abilities. The essence thus serves as both a toolkit and a guiding force, pushing its bearer toward a specific, vampiric combat identity.

## Skills

The Vampire (Guardian) Essence grants four skills that define its combat utility. Each skill plays a distinct role in the bearer's arsenal.

### Source of Darkness

A passive skill that influences the bearer's equipment choices, favoring gear that synergizes with dark-aligned abilities.

### Sacrificial Mark

An active skill that allows the user to mark targets, likely for strategic or debuff purposes.

### Immortal One

An active skill that grants temporary immortality or enhanced survivability, reflecting the vampire's undying nature.

### Blood Master

An active skill that enables manipulation of blood, either for offense, defense, or healing.
