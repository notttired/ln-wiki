The Bone Knight Essence is a Rank 7 monster essence obtained from the Bone Knight, a creature encountered in the Demon Worship Hall. It bears the Yearning attribute, which grants it a unique focus on a single active ability rather than a broad set of powers. The essence provides substantial enhancements to physical stats such as Cutting Power (+12), Bone Density (+55), Agility (+15), and Endurance (+15), but it also imposes penalties to Flexibility (-7) and Height (-25), significantly reducing the user's stature. These trade-offs make it a specialized essence suitable for certain combat styles.

Ainar claimed the essence after Bjorn secured it from the Bone Knight's remains in Chapter 32. Upon using it, Ainar gained the Life Drain ability, allowing him to sap vitality from foes, as well as the Preservative passive, which halves the severity of wound-worsening effects. The essence's Yearning nature means it concentrates all its power into this single active ability, making it straightforward but potent in the right hands. The reduction in height is a notable side effect that may affect mobility or reach.

## Properties

The Bone Knight Essence grants a set of stat bonuses and penalties: Cutting Power +12, Bone Density +55, Agility +15, Endurance +15, Flexibility -7, Height -25. It is a Yearning-type essence, meaning it provides only one active ability but compensates with powerful passives.

## Abilities

The essence grants two abilities due to its Yearning attribute, one active and one passive.

### Life Drain

The user drains life force from enemies, converting damage dealt into health recovery.

### Preservative

A passive ability that halves the effect of wound-worsening effects, reducing the risk of critical injuries.
